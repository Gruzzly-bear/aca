Only known photo of the American Secure Containment Initiative's Omega-7 facility.

CLASSIFICATION COMMITTEE MEMORANDUM

A Multiplex-class anomaly is a group of two or more discrete anomalies that are inextricably linked, to such a degree that describing them separately is either inadvisable or impossible.

When reviewing the database file of a Multiplex-class anomaly, please take care to read all available sections completely. Failure to do so may result in disciplinary action.

—Dir. Jean Karlyle Aktus, USINBL Site-81
