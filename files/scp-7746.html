Item #: SCP-7746

Object Class: Thaumiel

Special Containment Procedures: Dreams and their dreamers are to be ranked by productivity within SCP-7746. Low-productivity residents are eligible for removal.

Individuals are to sleep no more than 30 hours in SCP-7746 to avoid extraphysical neuron displacement. On-site personnel may sleep for up to 45 hours (opt-out). Nanoscale somnosensors and sedatives will be injected during sleep.

Containment supervisors are to act as church staff and clergy. Repeat users are to be catalogued and allowed inside. Foundation assets within the Brussels Police Service are to covertly expedite entry and sleep within SCP-7746. Asset secrecy is to be maintained.

Description: SCP-7746 is a small wooden church in Brussels, AB, CA. It was owned by the McClure family from 1895-2020 until being bought by Foundation front company Ranger Developers Inc.

SCP-7746 generates valuable human training data. Latent inforrhea is transcribed into the structure upon submission. New formations overwrite older generations, making extensive records necessary. An estimated ███,███,███ phantom neurons are currently firing within vital structural fibers.

Reports of excessive sleep and visible dreaming began in 1994. SCP-7746 became a local attraction wherein the public would record friends' and family members' dreams. The church hosted numerous gatherings and events until Foundation investigation of the property began in 2000. Locals amnesticized.
