Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8239 is to be kept in its containment chamber at Site-██ at all times. Testing SCP-8239 without permission from Level 4 Personnel or project leader Dr. Wales is strictly forbidden. Doing so will result in severe punishment.

Description: SCP-8239-A is an Apple II Plus model PC, with "tribute" crudely scribbled on its right side with permanent marker. When the console command "████████" is entered, SCP-8239-A will "generate" a database file corresponding to no known SCP in the database. Five (5) seconds after the file creation, an SCP-8239-B instance will manifest.

SCP-8239-B instances are anomalies that correspond to their created SCP-8239-A file. These anomalies can range from harmless to severely dangerous. These instances demanifest around three (3) minutes after their manifestation.

Recovery Log: SCP-8239 was discovered in Dr. ████████'s office at Site-██. SCP-8239 was collected by Mobile Task Force Mu-41 and contained for testing of anomalous properties.

A piece of paper was also found taped on the top side of SCP-8239-A.2

Designation: SCP-8239-B001 - "Calcium Stephen King"

Description: SCP-8239-B001 is a mechanical puppet named "Calcium Stephen King". When a civilian woman named Sarah dies in front of SCP-8239-B001, it will deanimate your right shoulder.

Tested: D-8239-1 (real name, "Sarah █████████") brought and killed in SCP-8239's chamber. Researcher ██████'s right shoulder ceased function.

Note: What the hell? - Researcher ██████

Designation: SCP-8239-B002 - "Arnold Fitzwilliams Clone"

Description: SCP-8239-B002 is a clone of Arnold Fitzwilliams, capable of bending reality anchors. SCP-8239-B002 gets sexual gratification from doing this.

Tested: Not tested

Note: No. Just, no. - Dr. Wales

Designation: SCP-8239-B003 - "The Files"

Description: SCP-8239-B003 is an archive of SCP-447 tests involving the Mexican entity "Dr. Chung". When a subject spends ten seconds reading SCP-8239-B003, they will dance and suffer.

Tested: D-8239-2 told to read SCP-8239-B003 documents. After ten (10) seconds, D-8239-2 lost control of their movement. When questioned, D-8239-2 claimed they were in "extreme pain".

Note: There is no recorded "Mexican" entity named "Dr. Chung." - Dr. Wales

Designation: SCP-8239-B004

Description: SCP-8239-B004 is an amoeba located in your eyes. SCP-8239-B004 begins increasing in size and ultraviolet intensity. It will continually kill you.

Tested: D-8239-3 brought into containment chamber and exposed to SCP-8239-B004. Subject then began to [REST OF LOG REDACTED]

Note: All Foundation personnel involved in the SCP-8239-B004 experiment have been given Class C amnestics.
