SCP-9433

Item #: SCP-9000-ARC

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: In storage at Site-28. SCP-9000-ARC is held in a locked shipping container at all times; the container is filled with biodegradable loose-fill packing in the event of relocation.

Description: SCP-9000-ARC is a concrete statue, spray painted white with Krylon brand paint. It depicts an androgynous nude figure, reclining while pointing to the north.

Origin: SCP-9000-ARC was purchased from GoI-012 ("Marshall, Carter and Dark Ltd.") during an auction in Italy. According to GoI-012, it was designed and produced by its defunct in-house art department. Internal records on its production were lost in the intervening years; there is apparently controversy within GoI-012, concerning whether it is a depiction of St. John the Apostle or the Virgin Mary (or, a composite entity). It was purchased for 447.60 pounds.
