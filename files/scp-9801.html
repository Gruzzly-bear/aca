SCP-9801

Item #: 9801

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: The contents of this file are not to be read except in case of encroaching large-scale noöspheric convergence. Neither are the individual facts in this file to be communicated with another individual, nor stored in any external medium except a human brain. Any such stored information is to be destroyed. After following procedure LÖNNROT, personnel reading this file are to be amnesticised immediately.

For their own safety, personnel are to avoid considering the contents of this file, including these containment procedures, in depth.

Procedure LÖNNROT is as follows:

1) 4 personnel are to be chosen as observers. These are to be selected for (in order of priority):
- Poor verbal comprehension
- Poor ability to follow complex implications
- Poor grasp of the English language specifically.

2) 16 different personnel, the participants, are to each read at most 2 randomly-chosen items in the description below, and then list:
- One potential fact that may be inferred from the 2 facts they were provided.
- A potential containment protocol that may be of use against an anomaly implied by the inclusion of the 2 provided facts.

3) These personnel are then to be amnesticised and to read 2 randomly selected outputs (i.e. both fact and protocol) from the previous cycle, and repeat the process. At each step, protocols alone are to be submitted to observers. If at any iteration two or more suggested protocols are deemed explicitly contradictory by observer personnel, that iteration is to be declared invalid.

4) After 5 repetitions of this cycle, all suggested containment protocols are to be executed regardless of political, monetary, or personnel expenditure.

5) All notes are then to be destroyed, not copied anywhere thereafter under any circumstances, and all participants amnesticised.

The combined containment protocols have been found to contain, as a subset, effective measures against SCP-9801 in 99.96% of simulated case studies.

DO NOT PROCEED PAST THIS POINT UNLESS ENGAGING IN PROCEDURE LÖNNROT.
