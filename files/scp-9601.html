Subsection of SCP-9601-1 instances upon manifestation.

Dale Florens.

Special Containment Procedures: All 531,094 surviving SCP-9601-1 instances have been relocated to wildlife reserves across the planet1. The 468,906 deceased SCP-9601-1 instances have been donated to philanthropic organizations dedicated to feeding the impoverished.

Given the irregular nature in which SCP-9601-1 instances initially manifested, further containment has been deemed unnecessary. SCP-9601 has been adequately disposed of.

Description: SCP-9601 refers to a 16th century Persian oil lamp, the bottommost shards of which contain a transcription that reads as follows:

Shine the lamp,
Wield its power,
Make the wish,
Of your special hour.

SCP-9601-1 is the overarching designation given to 1,000,000 Male2 White-tailed Deer (Odocoileus Virginianus) which spontaneously manifested above the property of 47 year old Dale Florens in Summer Springs, VA, on November 25, 2025 at 14:39.

Both Dale Florens and SCP-9601 were instantly pulverized beneath the weight of multiple SCP-9601-1 instances.
