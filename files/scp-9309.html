SCP-9309

PoI-91741 with a portion of SCP-9309-1. Note the lack of deterioration.

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9309's original form is to be confined to Confinement Cell P-S-H in Site-93. SCP-9309 possesses Level 0 Clearance, which is contingent upon its continued cooperation.

Personnel are not to disconnect SCP-9309 from Foundation intranet. In the event of SCP-9309-1's complete degradation, SCP-9309 will be reclassified as Neutralized.

Therapeutic services are available, on request, to SCP-9309. These have gone unused so far.1

Description: SCP-9309 is was a life-size, sapient sculpture strongly resembling classical depictions of the Hindu Puranic hero Dadhichi.2 SCP-9309 is formed from non-anomalous bronze with a hollow chest cavity, and is sculpted in a sitting, contemplative posture. SCP-9309 is immobile and impervious to all forms of damage, though this indestructibility notably does not extend to SCP-9309-1.3

SCP-9309-1 has displayed the capacity to, when placed within SCP-93094:

  • Verbal communication with Foundation personnel.
  • Perform accurate feats of prediction. It is currently unknown5 if this involves predicting the future or temporal manipulation of yet-to-occur event probability.
  • Perform complex statistical calculations, including some forms of anomalous math.
  • Play complex games via verbal guidance, ranging from chess to real-time strategy games such as Starcraft 2.6
  • Generate effective advice on topics it is unaware of. When applied to Foundation processes, these suggestions have uniformly improved containment, workflow, and reduced interpersonal conflict without failure.

If SCP-9309-1 is removed, SCP-9309 becomes inanimate7; however, testimony collected from SCP-9309 after replacement of SCP-9309-1 indicates that it retains awareness during this time. Further, SCP-9309-1 is nonfunctional unless inside SCP-9309, making separation of these two components useless.

There's the gist of it. And it's probably why you're here, of course. It's edit approval day, right? I've added a good sized one there about everything that's been happening, and I hope it's educational!

Please give it a read. If you have any questions, give your cursor a shake. I'll be here.

SCP-9309 has been wildly beneficial to Site-93 since its containment and usage in a professional setting. As a result, it was rewarded with Level 0 clearance, the highest level of clearance offered to non-Foundation employees. To further maximize potential assistance, it was suggested that SCP-9309's consciousness be transferred to a digital state.

Foundation technomancers managed to establish an analog-digital connection between SCP-9309-1 and Site-93 servers. This successfully allowed SCP-9309's consciousness to travel from SCP-9309 to Site-93 intranet, at which point SCP-9309 (adjustment of designation pending) was given limited access to Site-93 files, programs, and applications, as well as the wider Internet to assist with future datacrawling tasks.

SCP-9309 has adjusted to this new state seamlessly. Anomalous effects improving productivity continue to apply in spite of its digital state. Site-93 engineers have informally christened SCP-9309 as "dadhichi.aic."8

Discovery

SCP-9309, in spite of its invulnerability, displays signs of natural degradation. Analysis of soil composition collected from rusted portions of SCP-9309 suggests that the statue's origin is on the greater Indian subcontinent, and dates back at least 2000 years. Further information regarding the production of SCP-9309-1 could not be determined; however, small etchings iconography resembling known Indian Groups of Interest were present on the two largest gears. Testimony from SCP-9309 indicates it was primarily used in a school setting to function as a substitute for a history teacher.

SCP-9309 was acquired on June 14th 2014 following a raid on the home of PoI-91741 Victor Thrombey, age 76, in Milan, Italy. Thrombey is a known collector of anomalous art, and Foundation agents were notified of his acquisition of SCP-9309 as part of a lot of 32 items from an unknown source. PoI-91741 had been utilizing SCP-9309 for investment advice.

Upon discovery, SCP-9309 was found to have been crudely attached to a WiFi router via use of electrical tape and an Ethernet cable wrapped around its waist. This did appear to successfully connect SCP-9309 to the Internet9, allowing SCP-9309 unbridled access to large amounts of content hosted on the world wide web, including content left unindexed by search engines.10

This significantly increased SCP-9309's computational abilities, and analysis suggests this connection to be responsible for its rapid pace of educational and identity development.

At the time of discovery, PoI-91741 was talking to SCP-9309 while wielding a golf club. Though his original question is inaudible, in response SCP-9309 spoke aloud in English:

To protect SCP-9309, PoI-91741 was disarmed and detained, and SCP-9309 was contained without protest. Both SCP-9309 and SCP-9309-1 were recovered in what was thought to be intact condition; however, this was not confirmed on-site.

Analysis

SCP-9309-1 was already significantly damaged by the time of recovery, and is expected to completely succumb to this degradation by 2030 at the latest. In spite of its digitized state, this is expected to render SCP-9309 effectively Neutralized. Foundation engineers are currently attempting to reverse this process, though attempts have been unsuccessful. SCP-9309 has been notified of the possibility of failure. Though Foundation outlook is positive, SCP-9309's is now strongly optimistic also, or is trying to be!
