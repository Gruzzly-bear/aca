SCP-9813

Item #: SCP-9813

Object Class: Thaumiel

Special Containment Procedures: Field agents operating in areas of heightened resistance may be issued SCP-9813 items to be carried on their persons' for the purposes of pacification of nearby individuals. Agents will be provided instructions of feeding times with portioned containers of liquid feed prepared ahead of operations.

Agents are instructed to not read the items. Chips planted in the dermis of the covers will inform of any attempt to open and examine them.

SCP-9813 items not currently in use are to be housed individually in Safe-Class Small Organism Containers housed at Site-19.

Description: SCP-9813 designates ██ organisms which resemble King James English translations of the Christian holy text known as the "Bible." The covers are composed primarily of chitin with microscopic analysis of the pages showing they are similar in structure and composition to silk as is produced by bombyx mori (Domestic Silk Moth). Mouth parts are present at the top of the spine which also allow the item to expel waste.

The scriptural contents of SCP-9813 are consistent with other prints of the King James English translation, with the addition of excerpts from original manuscripts in Latin at relevant portions alongside both classical and contemporary commentaries also findable in mundane prints of the period. As such, ███ instances of since classified information is present.

Research into genetic alteration of SCP-9813 items to bring this information in line with the Sinai Protocol is ongoing within the Department of Continuity.

Coloration of the covers proports the items were printed at Oxford University from the Clarendon Press in the year 1832. Three names are listed as having been the printers responsible however no such individuals appear to exist in surviving records from that period. Coloration on the inside of the back cover bears the signature of one "Sir ██-███████" alongside the following; "For our brothers in Christ in India. May the savages come to see the light and mercy of God." Investigations have determined from surviving records that Sir ██-███████ was a known occultist and shareholder of the British East India Company. Further information appears to have been lost during early enactment of the Veil Protocol.

When a sleeping subject is within a 9-meter proximity of an SCP-9813 item they will begin to experience vivid dreams and be rendered catatonic for the duration thereof. This duration is dependent on the strength and contents of a given subject's beliefs prior to exposure. Belief systems that are irrational or which encourage the subject to act more aggressively have been shown to result in longer durations of resultant dreams. Individuals who report to be atheists are not immune to this phenomenon. Removal from SCP-9813's presence has shown to have no effect on the duration or contents of resultant dreams.

Dreams resulting from exposure to SCP-9813 have been reported as universally traumatic. Events depict the subject or others known to them being at the epicenter of an event important to their belief system blended with motifs and imagery from Christian mythology. Commonly this is violent in nature. With the subject or those others known to them being exposed to repeated harm whilst being forced to submit confessions regarding various transgressions committed either individually or collectively, make accusations against others, and renounce previous beliefs. These dreams have been shown to make subjects more docile and open to suggestion in the days immediately proceeding exposure.

Discovery: SCP-9813 was first discovered during investigations into a spike of assault cases and drug use in [REDACTED], Louisiana, United States in the summer of 2014. Reports from agents embedded into state law enforcement indicated that residents of the area showed consistently impaired cognitive functions with the majority of subjects lacking any decision-making capacity. Subjects also exhibited archaic and extreme theological positions out of line with those dictated by the Sinai Protocol which caused them to clash with law enforcement at every encounter.

The town of [REDACTED] was determined to a be a rural community ██ km southeast of the city of Lafayette where much of the residents had been split between feuding churches that all claimed the others to be theologically corrupt. The preachers of two of the churches and the wife of the third were discovered to all be resistant to the influence of SCP-9813 items, which they were in possession of.

The interrogation log of one of these subjects has been appended below.

In the end, a total of ██ SCP-9813 items were recovered from [REDACTED]. Given the prolonged exposure the residents had to the items and information they contained, amnestics were deemed to be an inadequate measure of cover up. Instead, the local sheriff's office was replaced with Foundation agents and the process of incrementally depopulating the town was initiated.

Subjects which showed minimal cognitive damage were subjected to memetic conditioning and folded into other civilian populations. Subjects which retained at least basic functions were taken on as low level staff in facilities around the area. Remainder were folded into D-class population.

Addendum 9813-1: From 13 to 21/11/2014 information was incrementally provided by staff acquired from [REDACTED] regarding what was determined to be the locations and concentration of additional SCP-9813 items.

When questioned how this information was obtained and why it was not offered previously subjects expressed a mixture of confusion and distress. Through use of mnestics and advanced interrogation techniques, multiple subjects confirmed that the information had been obtained in dreams. It was also determined that the dreams of these subject now permanently resembled those caused by SCP-9813 items.

Following the information provided, a British Occult Service facility was discovered in the outskirts of the rural village of Ty-Mawr in the United Kingdom. It was quickly evident that a conflict had occurred some hours before the arrival of Foundation agents with the presence of corpses, running vehicles, ongoing fires, and damage to furnishings both on the top side and within subterranean levels. Mixed among the bodies of Service agents were those of plain clothed individuals armed with submachine guns and possessing of various narcotics. Tattoos of a tree symbol were present on the backs of their right hands indicating █ ██████ ██████████ ██ ███ ███████ ██████████. Notably, some corpses of Service agents showed evidence of wounds inflicted by 5.56x45mm ammunition.

Locked in a vault on the bottom floor of the facility was ██ additional SCP-9813 items along with various documents of varying levels of legibility. A single surviving Service agent was present, identified as Timothy Henderson, who showed signs of cognitive degradation. He was only minorly injured with a stanched gunshot wound in his arm. Questioning yielded no information as the agent was only muttering the phrase "Matthew 18-6"2 repeatedly.

Most other documents within the facility were found to have been burned or otherwise rendered destroyed/illegible. Computer systems were wiped, including security feed footage, with a number of hard drives discovered smashed or on the bodies of the plain clothed corpses.

Data recovery teams were able to salvage information that points to the use of SCP-9813 items on nearby populations by the British Occult Services and the recall of all such items in their possession by the director of this facility in October under unclear circumstances. It is suspected additional items remain in the possession of the British Occult Service.

Addendum 9813-2: Attached are select portions of Document 9813-C, which was recovered during the raid on the British Occult Service facility in Ty-Mawr. The document appears to have been a digitized then printed copy of numerous letters exchanged between employees of the British East India Company sometime around 1843 given references to attacks made by member of the ████████ tribe. The document details early deployments of SCP-9813 items within territories controlled by the company.

With examination of the full journal, as well as documents located in reference to it, it would appear the item was recovered from a village in West Bengal sometime in the 1890s during a conflict between the then nascent SCP Foundation and a section of the Sepoy people who had █████████ ████ █ religious sect ███████ ██████ █ collection of brahmin possessing ███████ ███████ which allowed for the ██████████ of their █████ into the noosphere and across the █████ ██ ██████. Notably, no SCP-9813 items were documented among materials recovered in this same period.

The population is documented as having been wiped out in their near entirety. However, they are also documented as having appeared again during the climax of the Indian Independence Movement in the late 1930s as well as during the Sino-Indian War in 1962. In the former case interfering with Foundation operations in India and in the latter providing intel to left-wing extremist groups against the Indian government.

It has been deduced that the group at this time largely operates within the noosphere. Whether this group constitutes an entity entirely independent from the ███████ ██████████ or a semi-autonomous branch of it is unclear.

Authors: Dr. █████ Kavanaugh & Dr. █████ Murray

Goals


To ensure the American political and economic establishment remains within the control of the Foundation so as to facilitate smooth containment and the persistence of normalcy, the objective of this operation is to shift American religious beliefs to a form amenable to Foundation goals and preferably also within a shape which is easy to be controlled by Foundation operatives.

This may be done by the alteration of existing religious institutions, as has been accomplished through the Sinai Protocol and similar existing activities; or through the creation of new religious movements. Whichever is more accomplishable.

Methods

  • First, populations of heightened visibility will be identified by Foundation analysts. High population cities like the national or state capitals are most efficacious for this.
  • Second, insertion of agents equipped with SCP-9813 items into the population with instruction to carry the items on their persons at all times. Visiting of hospitals, hotels, and other areas of high resting activity is necessary.
  • Third, agents embedded into water treatment facilities are to introduce Class G amnestics to the drinking water. Individuals not exposed to SCP-9813 will be made similarly susceptible to the insertion of new ideas and will likely follow along as those that were exposed fail to resist the following measure.
  • Fourth, insertion of memetic vectors into broadcasts and prints which will gradually shift beliefs of the targeted areas towards Foundation desired doctrines.

It is hoped through these measures that general American political thought can be narrowed into forms acceptable for Foundation goals.

Expected Complications


Given repeated intervention attempts against the use of SCP-9813 by members of the ███████ ██████████, primarily those now designated by the Foundation as the Sepoy Dreamers, it is likely that the measures employed as part of this will be uncovered by them and efforts will be made to hamper our agents and likely to disrupt the veil.

As such, heightened information security measures will need to be taken in targeting cities. Sabotage of internet services under the guise of maintenance or outages will restrict the capacity for hostile elements to spread awareness of Foundation operations. Dissemination of backdoor software that would allow us to monitor electronics activity on numerous devices simultaneously with the assistance of Artificial Intelligence Constructs would also be of use in this effort.

Mobile Task Force elements disguised as police should remain available to support agents during any outings. Also, given the effects of Class G amnestics when used on such large scales, it is likely that amid our efforts heightened social tensions will arise. Given the ongoing election cycle already disrupting cohesion among the populace, fighting among civilians is likely. Detainment of especially violent individuals and their specific exposure to SCP-9813 may help mitigate this.

The operation was approved in September of 2015 and carried out over the winter in 6 American cities those being Albuquerque, New Mexico; New York City, New York; San Diego, California; Wichita, Kansas; Tampa, Florida; Baton Rouge, Louisiana; and Arlington, Texas. While not the only measure taken to influence the results of that election, it has been considered a success as the nominations of Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump Jr. for the Democrat and Republican parties respectively eliminated much of the threat previously posed by other candidates.

However, the operation did result in █ SCP-9813 items being lost in Tampa, alongside the agents who were charged with handling them. They are presumed destroyed. While the culprits were not identified, it is suspected the Sepoy Dreamers played a role in this

It has since been determined a defecting agent with connections to the Sepoy Dreamers was responsible for these actions. All lost SCP-9813 items have been recovered, along with the agent responsible following their capture in [REDACTED], Iran on ██/05/2016. A portion of the interrogation log of this agent has been attached here due to insights it has provided into the reach, motives, and goals of the Sepoy Dreamers which should be kept in mind for operations going forward.

Interviewed: Eustice Connard

Interviewer: Dr. ██████ Madina

Foreword: For the purposes of this interrogation, former Agent Connard was kept in a state of only semi-lucidity with the help of various chemical solutions thus allowing him to be actively questioned whilst also being subjected to the effects of an SCP-9813 item. Such allowed for the study of its effects on his cognition and memory retention whilst also warding against intrusions by members of the ███████ ██████████.

Contents of Connard's resultant dreams were obtained through the use of equipment outlined in Document 9813-4B.

<Begin Log>

[Audio recording devices can pick up Connard mumbling prayers to himself. He has been hung up by his ankles from the ceiling with his bound hands hanging beneath him. There is a soft dripping sound of his sweat falling to the cell floor. He is blindfolded but monitoring equipment reports that he is perceiving the interior of Site-19 which has been redesigned to bear architecture and decorations reminiscent of those described among accounts of the first temple of Jerusalem. Individuals are present, dressed in vibrant cloaks and tunics with faces hidden beneath golden bull masks. Each holds a stone and a whip. Each demands a question that cuts into Connard's ears like hot knives.]

Connard: [whispering] And it shall come to pass, as soon as I am gone from thee, that the Spirit of the Lord shall carry thee whither I know not; and so when I come and tell Ahab, and he cannot find thee, he shall slay me: but I thy servant fear the Lord from my youth.

[Connard slips through the halls of the false Site-19. Nude and exposed. Thirsting and in pain. Blood is left in a trail behind him like the mucus of a snail. Armored men and women with radiant faces crowd around him as he moves. Audio recording devices pick up a panicked struggling sound as one runs a sickle-shaped sword along the skin of his back. An accusation is levied against him that is not recorded.]

Connard: And Elijah said, As the Lord of hosts liveth, before whom I stand…

[The door to the interrogation cell is pulled open and the tapping of dress shoes on the concrete nears Connard. His breathing can be heard hitching as his dreams shift and lose coherency for some moments. When they return, he lies atop a sacrificial altar. His legs and arms too weak to move. Cloaked figures surround him. A crackle of thunder comes down from the clouds above. White glowing aircraft are visible circling above like vultures. He turns his head to the left and sees the statue of a golden calf high atop a burning pyre of filing cabinets and human bodies.]

Connard: [stammering] Elijah took twelve stones, according to the number of the tribes of the sons of Jacob, unto whom the word of the Lord came, saying, Israel shall be thy name.

[A radiant figure slowly lowers from the clouds above. It hurts to look upon and makes Connard visibly wince. Audio recording devices note the sounds of a chair being moved.]

Dr. Madina: Connard. You're nearing hour 8 of this. You won't survive much more if the medical readings are anything to go off.

Connard: Lord God of Abraham, Isaac, and of Israel, let it be known this day th-

Dr. Madina: Shhhhhhh, enough of that Connard. I'm going to ask you some questions. You answer them to my satisfaction and this ends. If not, we'll keep going till there's nothing left to be questioned.

Connard: P-please. Please stop it.

[There is a soft clattering of metal followed by a zipping sound as an Advanced Interrogation Kit is laid out on the floor beside Connard. In his dream, the radiant figure has rested atop him. He can hardly breathe. Speaking with Dr. Madina's voice, it reaches down to press the tips of its fingers into his side. The skin breaks immediately on contact.]

Dr. Madina: Let's check your memory first. Here's an easy question. How many disciples did Jesus have, and who was the first amongst them?

Connard: T-… Twel- ack!

[Audio recording picks up a slow and brief metallic sheen.]

Dr. Madina: Try again.

Connard: Fuh-fuck! Thirteen! There was thirteen! Starting with Peter. Brother of Andrew. Rock of his church, fisher of men!

Dr. Madina: Good, good. Where were you going with the SCP-9813 items?

Connard: I-India. Over near Ludhiana!

Dr. Madina: Why there?

Connard: Please, miss, I-I cannot say… Please…

[Dr. Madina makes another incision on Connard's side. In the dream the radiant figure has sunk its hand wrist deep into his abdomen. He can feel it grasping. He can see the smile beneath its shine. The laughs of a false prophet can be heard on the wind.]

Connard: Augh! I don't know! I swear! I just know they wanted to meet at a mass grave!3 I DON'T KNOW!

[LOGS OMITTED]

[Connard can be heard panting heavily through grit teeth. The dripping of blood on the floor has grown faster. In the dream the lashing of devils bearing the faces of dead site directors he had seen in old documents persists even as Dr. Madina takes her seat again.]

Dr. Madina: Next disciple Connard. Who were they?

Connard: [gagging] Nu-number… Number 7, Judas. The betrayer. Sold the Christ to the priests. Murdered by, number 8, Philip. God's sword bearer. Who built a temple upon his grave.

Dr. Madina: Yes. Why are they after SCP-9813 items? They have caused us problems in other areas before, but they seem particularly fixated.

Connard: Th-they… Think of them as abominations. These false bibles exist as monuments to the sins of man. Monuments that bend all thought around them into tedious uniformity and inescapable oppression. Oppression born in the mind. The oppression of fear and inferiority!

Dr. Madina: As opposed to the freedom of integration into the ███████ ██████████ overmind?

Connard: You… Know noth-

[A crunching sound is picked up by audio recording devices as Connard is struck. He begins to gasp and gurgle audibly. Dr. Madina can be heard pacing back and forth impatiently.]

Dr. Madina: Come on. Come on. Now, elaborate on what you mean, and watch your tone.

Connard: The… ██████████ seeks liberation through volition. Every- every part chooses to partake. Every dreamer is free to pursue what their mind desires. Every thought is facilitated freely for the sake of each other. What, the books do is by force. The books only take. For they are terrified of anything existing outside… defined, parameters. Exploitable… parameters.

Dr. Madina: We have other items that can allow such things. So, again, why are they so interested in these?

Connard: They… pursue the others… as well. These are just the most dire… that they know of, and can remove.

[LOGS OMITTED]

Dr. Madina: All right, last couple questions Connard then we'll send you on your way.

[Recording devices show that lucidity has almost entirely broken down in Connard at this point. The scene of his dream is rapidly shifting. In one he stands on a beach of steel beads watching Site-19 sink into a sea of blood. In another he is an Egyptian soldier, marching across the exposed bed of the Red Sea. In another, he is on a cross, with a weeping man to his right that calls for mercy from his father upon the jeering crowd of Foundation personnel]

Connard: I-I-I can't…

Dr. Madina: Come now Connard, you're right near the end. Would be a shame to come so far and to have to quit the race here. An easy question first, to jog your memory. The last disciple Connard. Who were they?

Connard: [mumbling] I don- fuck me thi- please God let me g-

Dr. Madina: The last disciple Connard.

Connard: Number 13. Andrew. Brother of Peter. Keeper of the Christ's secrets. Sent by him in heaven to retrieve something from the garden. Before… Before he was stopped by…

[Connard begins gargling as blood seeps down from his lips mixed with mucus and a bluish-purple fluid that Dr. Madina did not notice at the time. Recording equipment shows Connard's vitals spiking as the dream stabilizes around the vision of a radiant figure composed of fire standing before a silver and ivory gate. It begins to reach towards him.]

Dr. Madina: How many others in the Foundation have the dreamers reached out to Connard? Do you know?

Connard: You… You won't be able to snuff them out.

Dr. Madina: [annoyed] That's not what I asked Connard.

Connard: The ██████████, the Sepoy Dreamers, all… results. Emergent designs based on the same principles and driving factor. So long as there exists those to put words to freedom, who hate you, they will survive. So long as the human spirit has the capacity to recognize the suffering that is YOU, they will survive.

[Detection systems outside the interrogation cell pick up a sudden spike in EVE emissions emerging from Connard. Site security is alerted and attempt to contact Dr. Madina. She ignores the sounds from her walkie talkie and approaches Connard; crouching to speak to him face to face.]

Dr. Madina: Answer my question, Connard.

[Recording devices show the figure in Connard's dream has taken hold of him and pulled him into an embrace. The pain of the fire envelopes his whole body.]

Connard: Every moment of doubt, every fleeting thought of resistance. That's them. The dream of liberation. That's them. We declared war on impurity. On noncompliance and uniqueness.

Connard: You wanna know whose compromised? Who to be afraid of? Who will, at the first opportunity, turn on the Foundation to be free of this nightmare we made? Everyone.

[Audio feed cuts out as a sudden blast overloads the mic. According to video feeds jets of fire had erupted from Connard's eyes and mouth. Dr. Madina is immediately disfigured and begins to scream when security personnel enter the chamber.]

<End Log>

Closing Statement: Former Agent Connard was declared deceased immediately after the conclusion of this interrogation log. Investigations have surmised that a member of the Sepoy Dreamers or the wider ███████ ██████████ had somehow managed to breach thaumaturgic seals around Site-19 so as to euthanize Connard. Utilizing for these purposes his dreams as a medium through which to enact the ritual that terminated him.

The bluish-purple fluid that had seeped out of his mouth is still being subjected to examination. It was noted during a biopsy that the material had completely saturated former Agent Connard's brain. It is unclear how long this was present in his system prior to the interrogation or if it had developed over the course of such events.

Dr. Madina has made a full recovery from the injuries she sustained. Connard has also been removed from all Foundation records and increased surveillance has been placed on his surviving family.
