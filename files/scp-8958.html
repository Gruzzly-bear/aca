SCP-8958

Discovery location

Item #: SCP-8958

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8958 is contained in the Site-20 observation ward. Intravenous nutrition is approved, if possible.

Description: SCP-8958 is an amalgamated mass of human biological material, consisting of at least four individuals. Human skin covers the exterior, with hair, sensory organs and extremities protruding at irregular intervals.

SCP-8958 exhibits vital signs – perspiration, pupillary response, and sporadic convulsions – despite an anatomical configuration incompatible with life.

Discovery: SCP-8958 was discovered in a shipping container in Baltimore, MD. Evidence indicates the constituent individuals intended to immigrate illegally. No personal effects were found.

Updates:

11-25-24: Shortly after a custodian began disinfecting the chamber floor, matter spontaneously detached from the mass. It reformed into a female human, devoid of hair or distinguishing features, which identified itself as “Gabriela Victoria Ramos Silva”. It was designated SCP-8958-A.

Dr. Marissa Vo was assigned to interview.

11-27-24: SCP-8958-A claimed the disinfectant smell was reminiscent of its hospital in Caracas, though it remembered nothing else.

Vo: Sensory stimuli might be key.

12-10-24: Vo provided SCP-8958-A a cup of café con leche. Immediate changes were observed:

  • Height reduced ~5 in
  • Grew shoulder-length brown hair
  • Subject recalled additional memories

Of 49 tested stimuli, 3 produced results:

Stimuli Insights
Café con leche

Hospitalist in Venezuela for 11 years, left due to challenges related to U.S. economic sanctions.

Entered Spain legally, but visa expired while paperwork was pending.

Relatives in Florida claimed she might renew her medical license there. Discovered smugglers offering a cheap route via Rotterdam.

Met three others before entering shipping container. Names unknown – smugglers insisted on numeric codes – though one was from Afghanistan. No subsequent memories.
Ebony rosary

Subject was gifted a similar rosary by her mother, the day after revealing she was gay.

Subject kept the rosary, and reportedly became a doctor to restore her mother's pride.
Alma Llanera

"Alejandra… she decided this was our song.

My quinceanera was at her house. We danced on the edge of the crowd. Then we slipped into the shadows under the staircase and kissed.

We were together a few months. She wanted romance: gifts, big gestures. I've never liked those things.

I broke up with her in a letter, pretending to be heartbroken. In writing, it was easier to invent another version of me."

01-08-25: Following these tests, SCP-8958-A’s appearance matched those of official records for Silva, and the SCP-8958-A designation was retired.

Vo: Silva claims no memory of her amalgamation, but she exhibits nyctophobia and panic disorder, and complains of nightmares.

04-20-25: After 12 unproductive stimuli, Vo burned agarwood incense. SCP-8958 immediately exhibited intensified convulsions and diaphoresis, before extruding a male: SCP-8958-B.

05-10-25: Testing concluded:

Stimuli Insights
Kahwah tea

Farid Nasir Mohammadi: interpreter for U.S. forces in Afghanistan, 2004–2014. Promised U.S. visa, which was delayed by the 2017 Muslim travel ban. He was classed “security ineligible” for unknown reasons.

Following U.S. withdrawal, wife and brother were killed by the Taliban, and he fled the country.

Entered Latvia via Belarus. Denied asylum. Decided to attempt crossing to the U.S., hoping to complete his visa process after arrival.

Excerpt from Bani Adam:

"Human beings are parts of one body

In creation they are indeed of one nature."

 Subject taught U.S. soldiers the beauty of Dari poetry while living on the base, but found he enjoyed American movies more, and dreamed of living in California.
“Numb” by Linkin Park

"They played this constantly. I learned the words and sang along. They loved that.

One day, they wanted to search a mosque, and the imam said they must remove their boots. The soldiers were always impatient and I knew they wouldn't like it.

I wanted to impress them. I told the imam: 'Don't make trouble for us, or they'll think you're hiding something and take you away.'

He agreed… I knew he was afraid. I told the soldiers: 'I've negotiated for you – he's happy for you to search.'

When we left, I saw the imam's face, and I was ashamed."

Vo: Mohammadi identified the third individual as Layla Mahmoud Abu Khalil, from Palestine.

06-02-25: SCP-8958-C emerged from the mass after a bush of wild thyme was introduced to the chamber.

Stimuli Insights
Taboon with olive oil and za’atar

Raised on family-owned olive farm south of Nablus, in the West Bank.

In 2022, Israeli settlers burned the olive grove, poured concrete in the well and set fire to the home. Father was killed, family separated: fled with mother to UNRWA refugee camp.

Attempted to enter EU, and mother drowned during Mediterranean crossing. Learned of Palestinian diaspora in New Mexico and hoped to find agricultural work there.

Could not afford smuggling fee but agreed to repay debt on arrival.
Nabulsi soap

Subject was taught soap-making and embroidery by her grandmother. She learned their olive trees were over a century old, and that she was part of an ancient, unbroken chain.

Subject often reflected on this idea, finding it a source of strength and determination.
Tatreez embroidery

"When I was young, a goldfinch hit our window and broke its wing. My father said, 'Leave it, habibti, it's better not to interfere.'

I gathered the bird in my dress, and hid it in the shed. I bound its wing and nursed it. I liked having something that belonged only to me.

Then one day, it flew away. I cried for days, but couldn’t tell anybody why.

In time, I felt differently. Whenever I saw a goldfinch perched on an olive branch, it brought me happiness, because I knew it might be my friend."

Vo: Mohammadi and Khalil say the fourth individual spoke an unfamiliar Arabic dialect; they couldn't identify him.

07-04-25: Habeas corpus required Silva, Mohammadi and Khalil be released. Vo requisitioned ongoing surveillance; consequently, they are permitted to remain in the US, contingent on NDA consent and adoption of a cover identity.

12-26-25: Vo exposed SCP-8958 to 250 additional stimuli, observing only increased saccades and piloerection.

The remaining mass was moved to long-term storage.
