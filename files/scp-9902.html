Item #: SCP-9902

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: The entire town of Ptolemy, North Carolina has been quarantined. All residents have been peacefully evacuated to new living conditions in Raleigh under the pretense of a railway chemical spill.

All SCP-9902-B, including those belonging to civilians and Foundation staff, have been taken to Provisional Site-194 in the town center of Ptolemy. The resulting SCP-9902 is to be observed and collected for shipment to Site-59 in hermetically sealed canisters, where they will be kept in the Inert Storage Wing. All staff are advised to use Level 2 4 Anomalous Hazardous Materials PPE when handling SCP-9902.

No attempts to dislodge, alter, or destroy SCP-9902-A are to be made without approval from O5 Command. A series of refurbished graphing calculators have been placed at strategic points Provisional Site-194 beginning at 3.28¬†km radial point (approx. 10 meters away from the current border of SCP-9902-A). Should any of these devices produce SCP-9902, this would indicate an increase in the size of the area affected by SCP-9902-A. As this area constitutes a minor but ongoing CK-class reality-restructuring event, any changes to SCP-9902-A's zone of influence are to be reported to O5 Command immediately.

Description: SCP-9902 is a gelatinous substance believed to be extradimensional1 in origin. It is dark blue in coloration and carries an odor comparable to molten acrylic plastic with a hint of blackcurrant. Due to the fact that SCP-9902's odor is carcinogenic, further olfactory analysis is inadvisable.

The mass of SCP-9902 is indeterminate; identically-sized samples have weighed anywhere between 0.4¬†kg and 53.92¬†kg depending on factors such as ambient ultraviolet light, ambient temperature, and the phases of the Moon. (For a full list of mass-altering factors of SCP-9902, see Document-3829N.)

When SCP-9902 comes into contact with organic material, said material instantaneously transmutes into silicon, often taking the crystalline appearance of quartz or selenite. This process is extremely painful and irreversible for living subjects.

When SCP-9902 is exposed to a dead body, the effect on organic tissue remains the same. Under no circumstances is SCP-9902 to be allowed to come into contact with dead bodies.2

When placed within SCP-9902-A's electromagnetic field, any device with a microprocessor (i.e. modern electronic devices) generates a variable amount of SCP-9902 from whichever end of the device is opposite to its display ‚Äî or if there is no display, the input. This process occurs hourly, and cutting power from the device or removing it from the electromagnetic field does not reverse this. All such devices are heretofore designated SCP-9902-B.

SCP-9902-A refers to a device constructed by TotleighSoft in the town center of Ptolemy. The device has an indeterminate power source, and it's protected from tampering through three built-in 9mm autoturrets and a defensive coating of SCP-9902. SCP-9902-A produces an electromagnetic field over a 3.27¬†km radius that converts all devices with a microprocessor within into SCP-9902-B.

Additionally, while SCP-9902-A works on an internet connection, it is impossible to tamper with SCP-9902 electronically due to the TotleighSoft design philosophy of "Tacictal Unadvantsment" (s.p.) ‚Äî in other words, using hardware and software too unreliable and outdated to be hacked. SCP-9902-A, in particular, is a cluster computer of three Commodore 64s, four Sega Game Gears, and one LeapPad Learning System connected to Jay Jay the Jet Plane: High Flying Adventures.
