SCP-8690

Preview of SCP-8690 as displayed on major online video sharing platforms.

Special Containment Procedures: METATRON.aic must be used to monitor social media and online video sharing platforms for the detection and removal of any copy of SCP-8690.

One copy is to be kept in High-Yield Data Storage Facility 1 at Site-43; listening to the audio track for research purposes is permitted only after using anti-memetic filters.

If any of the audio tracks are listened to, the victim will experience a deep state of anxiety and anguish related to dramatic events that occurred between January 1, 1990 and December 31, 1999. Although the events may vary depending on the age and nationality of the listener, it has been found that the majority are linked to events that occurred in the United States and/or have had strong media coverage.

Addendum-8690: By further analysing the audio tracks, in particular by playing the song backwards, it was possible to find messages contained within the various songs.
