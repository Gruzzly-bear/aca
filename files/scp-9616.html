SCP-9616

Item #: SCP-9616
Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9616 is contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-188. The ventilation system of SCP-9616's cell is to be isolated from the general site ventilation. SCP-9616 is to be provided with a diet of meat and nutrients sufficient for an adult human, blended into a thin slurry and served in a wide bowl with a large available surface area. Deceased SCP-9616-A instances should be cleaned from the cell daily.

OKOLWE: Well, this is clearly out of date. Is there a reason it hasn't been updated?

KHANDAN: The new procedures are still waiting on Site-95's people to sign off.

OKOLWE: And the object class? Everyone needed to approve its upgrade to Keter is sitting in the room right now.

BENNETT: I'll action that tonight, Commissioner.

Description: SCP-9616 is a sentient and sapient swarm of between 3500 and 4000 anomalous Culex quinquefasciatus, commonly known as the southern house mosquito. SCP-9616 commonly forms a dense swarm in an appropriately humanoid shape. Individual SCP-9616 members, referred to as SCP-9616-A instances, live for approximately 2 weeks, performing a form of asexual reproduction at one week of age that produces a fully grown Culex quinquefasciatus. SCP-9616-A may be separated from the swarm, or may be formed into subswarms with SCP-9616 maintaining control over them, though this induces significant mental distress in SCP-9616. SCP-9616 is capable of vocalisation via synchronisation of wing-beats. Experiments have shown that a subswarm requires at least 140 members before it can vocalise in a way that is comprehensible to greater than 90% of listeners. When vocalising, separate subswarms all produce identical sounds and words. Toxicological testing on SCP-9616-A instances has not revealed any human-biocompatible blood-borne diseases.

SCP-9616 identifies itself as Andreas Katsaros, born 19 November 1981. Katsaros was reported missing from his home at ██ G█████ St, Dunedin, New Zealand on 03 August 1993. SCP-9616 has refused to elaborate on the circumstances of its disappearance, or how its anomalous properties initially manifested, only stating "I was always a pest." SCP-9616 was brought into foundation custody on 09 April 2000 after it presented itself to Goulburn Hospital, NSW, Australia.

UPDATE 18 MARCH 2007

SCP-9616 is capable of accelerating the reproduction of SCP-9616-A, allowing significant increases in volume and density at exponential rates. The rate of increase is constant across all subswarms and individual instances. If contained in a closed container, SCP-9616 is capable of increasing its density, and thus the pressure inside the closed container. The upper limit of this pressure is not known. In high-pressure scenarios, SCP-9616-A are compressed and liquefied. Liquefied SCP-9616-A are able to maintain reproduction of healthy SCP-9616-A for an unknown but finite amount of time, after which the liquified members become functionally deceased.

11 MAY 2005

Andy's depressive symptoms are becoming increasingly severe. I got permission from the site director to get him access to the LTSA3 common room, after Dr Khandan confirmed that he is limited to a roughly humanoid form. Had my first session in 3 months with Andy. The depression and agoraphobia have only gotten worse. According to the guards, none of them have seen him leave his cell in the last six weeks.
