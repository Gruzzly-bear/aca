SCP-8426 Investigation Transcript - Log 0

<Start Transcript>

Yarrow: Thorson, what are you talking about?

Thorson: Director, a series of systems failed on the 22nd, and after checking some logs, there were multiple ontological shifts detected.

Yarrow: Right. And that is…

Thorson: A bad thing.

Yarrow: How bad? You're the only one who noticed anything, so—

Thorson: I am also the only one at Site-33M who takes mnestics — and I can guarantee that something is different.

Yarrow: What changed?

Thorson: SCP-8426.

<A pause. Dir. Yarrow clears his throat.>

Yarrow: Remind me what that project is.

Thorson: Sir, I was hoping you would know.

Yarrow: Did we build it here?

Thorson: I don't know.

Yarrow: You don't know?

Thorson: That's what I've been trying to tell you—

Yarrow: What does the machine do?

Thorson: Again, sir, I do not—

Yarrow: What about the staff who worked on it. Can you find someone, and have them repor—

Thorson: Sir. That is what I have been trying to tell you; not a single person knows what SCP-8426 is.

Yarrow: To confirm: you are telling me that there is a machine that we may have built, in our Site — and we all know nothing about it?

Thorson: Yes.

Yarrow: How could this happen?

Thorson: I'm not sure yet, but I have a theory.

Yarrow: Which is?

Thorson: Somehow, I think the concept of what we made was forgotten.

Yarrow: That sounds like bullshit.

Thorson: That's what you said when you hired me.

<Silence.>

Thorson: Sir?

Yarrow: What do we need to figure this out?

Thorson: A full audit. We need to figure out what this does, who made it, and how it works — after all of that, then we can worry about why it changed.

Yarrow: And how are you going to do that?

Thorson: Very slowly.

<End Transcript>

Addendum 3: SCP-8426's Functionality and Purpose

Throughout the investigation into SCP-8426, there has been one common goal: understanding what SCP-8426 was built to do. SCP-8426 Activation Reconstruction

Step 1: Loading Material(s)

Due to the fact that (The following statement is probable): SCP-8426 is hollow and based on the (The following statement is probable): presence of material loading chamber(s), we suggest that the first step in activation was loading the material chamber(s). Due to the fact that (The following statement is probable): SCP-8426 is not a weapon, we can assume that (The following statement is probable): ammunition is not being loaded into SCP-8426; and given that component analysis suggests that (The following statement is uncertain): SCP-8426 does not create an additional end product, we believe that this (The following statement is probable): 'material' is the target of SCP-8426's functionality.

Step 2: Energy Generation

Given the presence of (The following statement is probable): capacitors, compensated pulsed alternators, and other intake connections, the next logical step in the operation of SCP-8426 is to (The following statement is probable): fill the capacitors, such that SCP-8426 can be activated. Given the (The following statement is probable): maximum electrical draw of SCP-8426 is 57 megajoules, it is clear that (The following statement is probable): SCP-8426 was designed to store, and then use significant amounts of energy per activation. It is (The following statement is uncertain): unclear how long said generation takes.

Step 3: Energy Application

Once SCP-8426 has collected enough energy, (The following statement is probable): that energy must be used in the activation. Via adjective comparison and component analysis and the fact that (The following statement is probable): SCP-8426's operating chamber has a significant range of operating temperatures, we suggest that SCP-8426's primary function is the (The following statement is probable): direct application of said energy to the 'material'. Furthermore, given the (The following statement is uncertain): continued traits revolving around heat or thermal energy, we suggest that (The following statement is probable): SCP-8426 applies thermal energy to the 'material' for a (The following statement is uncertain): given duration of time.

Step 4: Ejection

Once SCP-8426 has applied the thermal energy to the 'material', the functionalities and components that remain unused center around (The following statement is uncertain): the application of force, such that (The following statement is probable): a directional force is applied to the material. Due to the (The following statement is probable): lack of a dedicated output chamber, we believe that the (The following statement is uncertain): material is ejected from SCP-8426, post-thermal application. Upon ejection, an activation cycle of SCP-8426 is completed.
