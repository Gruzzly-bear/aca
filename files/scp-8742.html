SCP-8742

Item #: SCP-8742

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8742 is both uncontainable and self-obscuring, making the Foundation's role in the containment of SCP-8742 that of observation and record-keeping. Disappearances tied to known factors of SCP-8742 are to be investigated by Specialized Task Force Rho-7 "Rock Bottom" in order to curb suspicion. Recordings of SCP-8742 online are to be identified and destroyed.

Description: SCP-8742 is a recurring anomalous event that affects any location near water worldwide. SCP-8742 targets a single subject per event; no link1 between SCP-8742 subjects has been found.
The manifestation of SCP-8742 is tied to a series of circumstances, which has allowed the Foundation to identify SCP-8742 related deaths over time. These circumstances are listed below.

  • SCP-8742 events will only occur between the hours of 2 AM and 5 AM.
  • SCP-8742 will only trigger between sunset and sunrise.
  • SCP-8742 always affects subjects that are alone.
  • SCP-8742 is more likely to affect subjects with impaired coordination.2
  • SCP-8742 is more likely to affect subjects unfamiliar with the location.
  • SCP-8742 is always triggered immediately prior to or during a fall of any kind into the water.

SCP-8742 is the instantaneous translocation of an individual to the seafloor of their nearest ocean. The sudden shift in pressure will inevitably destroy the subject instantaneously, although their skeletal system will remain on the seabed due to the effects of a minor secondary anomaly that allows them to withstand the pressure.

Addendum-8742-A: Log of SCP-8742 Event.

The following footage was taken in Venice, Italy on July 6th, 2025.

Video Log — 06/07/2025

RECORDING START

The video camera is perched on the corner of a building, facing the canal below. There is very low visibility, provided almost exclusively by the few remaining lights on both sides of the bridge. The canal disappears into the dark.

A voice can be heard on the recording, growing louder as a man enters the frame. Foundation records identify him as 22 year old Walter O'Hara. He stumbles onto the street, and stops short of the canal.

O'Hara: (Singing.) Last night I drive to mumble city I thought about you, you were standing on the road yelling at the stop signs, the speed limit kept decreasing by ten—

The singing stops as he receives a call. He steadies himself on a nearby trashcan, and breathes in deeply. He puffs out his chest before answering the call. He smiles.

O'Hara: Talk to me, brother.

O'Hara: Oh, dude. Don't worry about it. Shit was gas. We had fun, didn't we?

He pauses for a moment, to hear what the person on the other line says. He looks around before replying.

O'Hara: Yeah man, I'm already at the hotel. Don't sweat it. Talk to you soon big dog.

He hangs up. He looks around for a bit, then takes out a single cigarette. He smokes a third of it before coughing, dropping it to the ground, and stomping it out.

Silence.

O'Hara: (Singing.) Throw your arms and throw, your arms. a-round me.

He walks over to the bridge. A quarter of the way through, he stops to look at the canal. He takes out his phone again, and dials a number. He put the phone on speaker, and then puts it up to his ear, anyway. He is swaying. The phone rings.

He stares ahead as it rings. The call does not go through. He sends a message, and resumes walking. Halfway through the bridge, he stops to take a picture of the sea.

The phone rings, and he answers it, puts the phone on speaker, and puts it up to his ear again. He covers his face with his hands for a second and takes a deep breath before talking.

O'Hara: Hey! Hey. How're you t'day ?

O'Hara: Oh! Oh, nah. Prob'ly a butt-dial. Issall fine, here, now, y'know? Miss you all. You all know.

O'Hara: Jussired 'sall, rocking it with Chris tonight we really just painted the town red ha you know us! I really want a cigarette right now though.

O'Hara: I know, I know. Can't smoke 'em ir you'll know. Here. Love you all, for real.

O'Hara: Anyway, bye.

The call disconnects. O'Hara is using both hands to grab onto the rail. He blinks a couple of times, and then lets go. He wobbles a bit before resuming his walk across the bridge.

He stumbles. He falls forward, onto the next step, face down, twisting onto his side as he falls. He hits the ground hard on his knees, but does not manage to stop his fall onto the next step.

He grabs onto the railing for a second, but his arm is unable to pull his weight up without his legs. He leans harder onto the side, inadvertently pushing himself over the railing.

SCP-8742 activates. Subject disappears from view.

RECORDING END



The remains of Walter O'Hara currently sit at the bottom of the Adriatic Sea.
