Special Containment Procedures:

This documentation is not to be accessed. Other means of containment are unnecessary, as the tools to achieve breakage will never be developed.

Description:

As of 2704, SCP-9704 has only manifested once. SCP-9704 was observed by David Green on December 1st, 2100. David Green was the last human to ever see Earth from space.

David Green was part of an experimental program1 which was launched under the guise of terraforming and colonizing other planets. He was sent alone. After leaving the earth's atmosphere, David Green willingly left his ship. He was not wearing a suit.

All gear brought on the expedition was lost. Further space travel has been uniformly outlawed. All thread devices have been incinerated. 99% of involved parties have been amnesticized. David Green traveled significantly faster than the speed of light.

The following images were transferred from his implanted ocular recording device2 between the years of 2100 to 5609. In anchored timescale the events transpired and the images were delivered over the course of 2 minutes. David Green was never recovered.
