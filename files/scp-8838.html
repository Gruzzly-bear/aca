SCP-8838

Item #: SCP-8838

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8838's wellbeing is currently the responsibility of Dr. Krista Eiko. Both reside in an off-site two-bathroom housing unit close to Site-19. No further action is required.

Description: SCP-8838 is Dr. Connor Hyland, a Level 1 researcher for the Animal Science Division, formerly employed at Site-19. On February 14th, 2026, the second-floor ceiling in the North Wing of the facility suddenly collapsed, resulting in three casualties. One of the deceased was SCP-8838, though his corpse would remain unidentified for a few hours, as the area was on the opposite side of his workspace.

He was interred in the regional cemetery. His body was wholly non-anomalous. On his possession was a box of Ferrero Rocher brand chocolates, all crushed. Due to secrecy protocol, no individuals were allowed to attend the burial.

Through unknown means, SCP-8838's consciousness has been transferred into the vessel of an IKEA-brand standing lamp within Site-19's fourth-floor employee lounge (see image). SCP-8838 possesses some control over the lamp's body and various functions, as well as limited locomotion via "waddling" with its base. However, it requires a constant power supply to remain conscious. SCP-8838 professes complete ignorance as to how or why its consciousness was transferred.

Despite the lack of any ocular, audial, or vocal orifice, SCP-8838 is capable of sight, hearing, and verbal communication. However, the only individual capable of understanding it is Dr. Krista Eiko, a Level 2 IT technician for Site-19. As Dr. Eiko submitted a request of work leave immediately following the collapse, this would remain undiscovered for several hours.

Addendum: Below are video surveillance transcripts taken from across Site-19, detailing the progression of SCP-8838's mental state following resurrection, as well as the actions of Dr. Eiko. Transcripts are divided between manifestation, discovery, containment, and the transfer process. Archival documents have been included where appropriate.

Eiko: Just admiring you.

Hyland: Ah…

[Silence.]

Hyland: You can come over, if you want.

Eiko: I guess I should do that, yeah.

[Eiko approaches Hyland. She turns her back to the wall and slumps down on the ground before bringing in her knees with her arms. Neither says anything.]

[Eventually, Eiko pulls out her phone to begin looking through her SCiPNET messages. Noticing this, Hyland shifts his dolly over to Eiko and turns on his light. Eiko looks up at him.]

Hyland: It's not good to read in the dark… I'm worried about your eyes.

Eiko: Oh. Um, thanks.

[Eiko looks down and shakes her head.]

Eiko: ..How are you holding up?

[Hyland fidgets.]

Hyland: It's strange… I feel like it still hasn't hit me that I died back there. I know it's been a week, but it doesn't feel like a week, because things still look the same. But then I try to move like a human, or take a look at you, and it all comes rushing back.

[He shakes his lamp shade.]

Hyland: They told me during the interview… after my burial, they went through all the post-termination processes. My mom, my dad, my friends and relatives…

[Hyland inhales deeply. He is attempting to stifle his crying.]

Hyland: (hoarse) They've been i-informed… of my passing… of c-course, the Foundation can't exactly tell them I came b-back to life, so… t-that's it, I guess.

Hyland: There's s-still a lot I have to get used t-to… but I will! I-I'll do it! I can't let it all wash over me again… I need to drill the truth into myself. I can't just keep coasting on my feelings like this, taking what comes so casually. Because that's not fair to you.

[Eiko looks up at him.]

Hyland: I-I've always had a problem trying to break out of my inertia… that's why I grew so connected to you. I could always tell… you wore your heart on your sleeve. You felt everything, so intensely. You weren't afraid to be raw to people…

[He inhales again.]

Hyland: Every time I visited you, even in 2025 when it was just the Valentine's chocolates, I always felt a burst… a burst of color into my life! They say I'm good at talking to people, and being easy to talk to in turn, but… that's not true! I'm j-just a people-pleaser who avoids all conflict…

[He fidgets once more. Unbeknownst to him, Eiko's eyes are starting to well up with tears again.]

Hyland: When I felt that attraction to you, I was inspired to break the cycle of my own life… that's why I went straight to the chocolate and messages. But still, it was so hard, and all I wanted was to go back into my bubble… and so we drifted apart.

[Hyland pauses for a moment.]

Hyland: I'm going to live. I'm going to feel it, feel those emotions like you. I'll cry, and I'll be raw, and I'll make sure I get reality into my head. I'll stop coasting and live… for your sake.

[Eiko covers her mouth with her hand.]

Eiko: Y-you're kidding me…

Hyland: Krista-

Eiko: Why on Earth are you blaming yourself?!

[Eiko stands up, angry. Hyland flinches. Eiko's expression suddenly collapses as she drops into her face and begins weeping.]

Eiko: Moron… Don't you get it!? It's my fault! I'm the one to blame!

[Eiko is now breathing heavily]

Eiko: Those flashes of color you're talking about? Those are the only times I stepped out into the world! I was why we drifted apart!

[Eiko clenches her teeth as she composes herself. She fails.]

Eiko: I thought I could avoid ever crying if I never had anything to cry over! That's why I studied computer science! That's why I got this job, so I never had to talk to people! I'd never have to… care…

[Eiko inhales.]

Eiko: I was so scared. My heart yearned for more, but I've always been so fucking scared. I didn't know what would happen if I lost the things most precious to me, and people and relationships felt so fragile…

[Eiko chuckles roughly.]

Eiko: Fear of the unknown… figures for a place like this, huh? I thought this position would validate my fears, because see! The world is frightening and unknowable and unpredictable and can snatch everything away in an instant! So why would I ever try?!

[Eiko sniffles in. She looks down to the ground.]

Eiko: B-But then came Luvenia, and then Ayumi, and then you, you all came into my life, and it was everything I wanted, and needed, but NO! I didn't want to risk it! I didn't want to risk feeling those emotions, not knowing what my own heart was doing! And so- and so- my fucking brain thought that I could just return to how it was! All I'd have to do was-

[Eiko inhales, attempting to subdue her crying.]

Eiko: …push you all away.

[She gulps.]

Eiko: I "knew" it was the right thing to do, and yet I found it so hard. Luv and Ayumi, I saw them less, and each time I did they were more worried than the last. God, they tried so hard to pull me out of my idiocy, but I never listened.

Eiko: You… it was a different kind of regret I felt for you. You were always so stable… you're basically the only person I ever felt truly calm around. The way you wrote, the way you spoke, it was always so level, so right. My heart wanted nothing less but to be around you, and yet my oh-so-logical brain didn't allow it.

Eiko: The less I saw your face, the more the regrets grew… and then came your 2025 Valentine's letter. I know you tried to word it as softly as you could, but… shit, that only made it hurt so much fucking more.

Eiko: I wanted desperately to fix it, but at the same time the nightmares wouldn't stop clawing at me… I kept putting it off. Summer, Halloween, the Christmas party, I thought I could end the spiral and reach you on the other side, but I was so terrified of falling, and eventually I thought I'd finally be able to talk to you when you came with the 2026 Valentine's chocolates, because you always came with the chocolates! I could always just procrastinate until then! I could always rely on Valentine's Day! Why even try something else?!

[Eiko is breathing rapidly now. She clutches her head with her hands.]

Eiko: And then… I heard the snapping in the roof… I heard the ceiling collapse right outside my office… I…

[Eiko quivers.]

Eiko: I rushed out… they'd already closed off the area, but… I could see your arm poking out from under the rubble, and a box of crushed chocolates… and I kept reloading the obituary page, hoping and hoping…

Eiko: …right up until I saw your name pop up. Black text on white background.

[Silence.]

Hyland: (softly) Krista…

Eiko: Ugh… Augh…

[Eiko collapses on her knees.]

Eiko: I'm the reason you were in that hallway. I'm the reason you died. I'm the reason… your parents think you died young…

Eiko: …and even then, the first thing you did when you came back… was try to make sure I was okay…

Eiko: I don't… deserve you… it's all… it's all…

[Sniffle.]

Eiko: IT'S ALL MY FUCKING FAULT!!!

[Eiko sobs profusely. Hyland shifts closer to make sure she is covered by his lamp shade.]

Eiko: It's all me… It's all me… I'm such… a horrible person…

Hyland: (softly) Hey, It's okay. It's okay. Let it all out.

Eiko: The only reason… I have you back… is all luck… otherwise…

Hyland: (softly) Hold on to my voice. I'm right here. I'm right beside you. You're not alone.

Eiko: Connie…

Hyland: (softly) It's me. It's Connie. It's me.

[Hyland continues consoling Eiko for the next couple of minutes. Given the secluded nature of the hallway, there are no passersby to interrupt them.]

[As Hyland keeps reassuring Eiko, she gradually sobs less and less, eventually being to stop completely. She wipes her tears off with her shirt sleeve and returns to a sitting position underneath Hyland's light. Both are silent once again.]

Eiko: I feel better now, weirdly. Like a weight's been lifted off my shoulders.

Hyland: I'm happy to hear that… crying is an effective way to relieve your stress.

Eiko: Hah, yeah, I remember that from school. Sorry for unloading all of that onto you. Might've been a bit insensitive.

Hyland: On the contrary, I'm… really happy that you trust me enough to be vulnerable like that! Besides, I told you my issues first. Though there is the issue of the security camera…

Eiko: Aw shit, I forgot about that. (cuckles) Fuck, this was the most I've ever said to someone in, like, years.

[Silence.]

Hyland: You're not a horrible person, Krista. Not in the slightest.

Eiko: Hard to believe otherwise sometimes…

Hyland: Hmm…

[Silence.]

Hyland: Okay, how about this? I… won't blame myself for everything?

Eiko: Huh?

Hyland: It's not fair for me to try and shoulder everything. It's rather insensitive myself. Things are so much more complicated, after all.

[Hyland's lamp shade turns to face Eiko.]

Hyland: …And now that I've taken this step, I hope you can follow me.

Eiko: W-What do you mean?

Hyland: You shouldn't hold all the blame either. We can figure this both out… together.

[Eiko stares at Hyland for a moment, before breaking out laughing.]

Eiko: You really are something, aren't you?

Hyland: In a good way?

Eiko: Absolutely.

Hyland: …Thanks, Krissy. That means a lot to me.

[Silence.]

Eiko: A second chance…

Hyland: What do you want to do?

Eiko: …I'll finally have the conversation I thought I'd never get to do.

[Eiko stands up and holds Hyland's lamp shade in her hands again. Hyland is visibly flustered.]

Eiko: I'm not putting off what I wanted to say anymore.

Hyland: A-a-ah?

[Eiko reaches down and grips the handle to Hyland's dolly.]

Eiko: We can go to my room, if that's okay with you.

Hyland: T-There's…

[Hyland's lamp shade twists nervously.]

Hyland: …no one I trust more than you.

[Eiko smiles softly. She pulls Hyland with her out of frame.]

+ Site-19 Dormitories, Log 2 (22/02/2026 9:45 PM)
- Hide Transcript

[The door to Dr. Eiko's dorm opens. The hallway light illuminates the otherwise dark room; it is in a comparable state to Log 1.]

Eiko: Sorry, I didn't have time to fix it up…

Hyland: That's fine. We can take care of it later, it's been a long day.

[Eiko moves Hyland to just left of the TV. She navigates her way over the clumps of clothing and other miscellaneous trash, arriving at the couch. She reaches underneath the blue coat.]

Hyland: Is that my old coat?

Eiko: Kind of. It's a new one I brought. Something else to remember you by, because reading your Valentine's still hurt.

Hyland: Oh…

[Eiko grabs something from the wedge of the couch.]

Eiko: But… that's all in the past now, right?

[She pulls out a small felt box, the contents unknown. She feels the lid flap with her thumb and then stuffs the box into her pant pocket. After a moment of silence, she turns back to face Hyland. She is smiling even more now.]

[She is silent as she watches Hyland. His light flickers enigmatically.]

Eiko: God… even as a lamp, you've always been so reliable. Cool metal and warm light, I can't think of anything more fitting for you. Fuck, I can't stop looking at you.

[She walks back to Hyland, holding his shade in her hands one more time.]

Eiko: Like a beach to my ocean, you were there to give me form. I always knew you were there, stable, strong, known… you never pushed me around like a cliffside, and you never folded away like so many grains of sand in the wind. You fit me like a puzzle, sometimes I wonder how I lived life before you.

[Eiko delicately rubs the shade's fabric.]

Eiko: (chuckle) My knight in shining light, I suppose.

[Quiet.]

Hyland: Cold ocean waters… you make me feel right. The wit of your words, the depths of your knowledge, they way you laughed, the way you spoke, I never thought someone could be so infinite… or rather, that I could see such an infinity in another person.

[His light grows brighter.]

Hyland: I can feel the shivers. They are the percussion that my heart and lungs need to finish their symphony. Even now when I look into your eyes, I feel the rush of wind invigorating my every inch, and I feel like I can face the world, as long as I have you by my side! I… I can't… I just so happy that I was able to live at the same time, in the same place, as someone like you!

[Sniffle.]

Hyland: I want to be a caretaker for the world, and sometimes I wonder what the point is! But then realize there's people like you in this reality, and I realize that it's all been worth it!

[Fidget.]

Hyland: You're so cool… you're so energetic… you're so soft… Ah, I can't figure out the words I want to say! I just… I can't take my eyes off you either!

[Hyland whimpers.]

Hyland: I don't know what this feeling is… I'm just so happy…

Eiko: Hey… I don't understand it either, but I'm happy too.

[Eiko brings Hyland in closer.]

Eiko: No, I'm ecstatic! Because you're alive.

[The two of them are audibly holding back tears. Several seconds pass as they remain still.]

[Eiko shuts her eyes, letting two small drops run down her face. She reaches into her pant pocket and pulls back out the felt box. She eyes it for a moment before opening the lid.]

[A replica diamond ring made of construction paper is within. The lid covers it from Hyland's view.]

Eiko: I have one more thing I want to say. A question. Are you prepared, o knight of mine?

[Hyland's shade twists nervously.]

Hyland: I don't know if this is presumptuous of me… but I think I already know what that question will be.

[Eiko glares at Hyland for a moment. She laughs.]

Eiko: And I think I already know your answer.

+ Site-19 Administrative Office (30/03/2026 █:██ PM)

Following the events logged above, Drs. Eiko and Hyland began official motions for the transfer of the latter's containment to the former. While the process typically would have taken several months — an issue compounded by the necessity to recognize an official engagement (and subsequent rapid marriage) between an anomaly and a staff member — additional assistance from Drs. Webb and Owston in getting the documents recognized and transferred allowed the majority of steps to be completed only one month.

By the end of March, the only steps remaining were official signoffs from two more members of senior staff. A final meeting to go over important forms was set up during this period.

[The office, located on Site-19's first floor, is a green-carpeted room with a wooden desk in the center. In attendance are Drs. Eiko and Hyland, discussing the final stages of form ratification with Site-19 Secretary Ragnar Kristo. Kristo sits behind the desk, a set of documents laid out in front of him. Eiko sits on the other end, embracing Dr. Hyland between her legs and arms, both folded. Given the nature of the meeting, site director Tilda David Moose is also present, watching from the corner.]

[Eiko and Hyland look ahead and nod along as Kristo moves through paper copies of (in order) the Personnel Special Character Evaluation Certification (PSCEF, more rigorous version of PCEF), Official Containment Transfer Request (OCTP, two copies), Official Foundation Marriage Approval Form (OFMAF, extended version), 2021 Specialized Human-Anomaly Interaction Ethics Module Completion Certificate, and 2023 Warranty of Damage to Anomalies (WDA), alongside various sub-forms. Eiko noticeably grimaces at the names of the last two.]

[The meeting takes in total three hours. Eiko and Hyland occasionally discuss certain elements of the transfer arrangement2 with Kristo. Eiko translates Hyland's statements when necessary.]

[Despite the short timeframe the process was finished in, all forms are determined to be complete and accurate. Kristo assures Hyland and Eiko that their part is completed, and recommends they begin packing for the move. After one final congratulation for their marriage, he nods to Dir. Moose, who proceeds to exit from the right door. After a moment, Kristo begins to follow behind her.]

[He passes through the doorframe. Begin relevant audio except.]

Eiko: Um, Mr. Kristo?

[Kristo pauses.]

Eiko: Do you think we'll be alright, in the end?

Kristo: Hm? What makes you say that?

[Eiko stutters.]

Eiko: Even now, it still feels like I'm trapped in a dream. Like things are too good to be true. I'm worried, that one day I'll wake up and it'll all be gone again…

Hyland: Honey…

Kristo: That's nonsense.

[Eiko and Hyland both turn to face Kristo.]

Eiko: How can you be so sure, though?

Kristo: What does Dr. Hyland think?

[Eiko looks back at Hyland, nestled between her arm and shoulder.]

Hyland: I feel the same way too, a lot of the time… I…

Eiko: (back to Kristo) He says he feels the same.

Kristo: There's the paradox. You can't both be dreaming.

[Eiko and Hyland look on, stunned. Kristo rubs the bridge of his nose.]

Kristo: You might think we live in a comedy, or a tragedy, but none of that's true. Our duty is anomalies, no? You can't expect the unexpected.

[Kristo looks up.]

Kristo: Sometimes that unexpected is truly awful, but sometimes it's a dream come true.

[Kristo turns back to the couple.]

Kristo: Sometimes the universe throws you a bone- or hell, a mountain of bones. There doesn't have to be a reason to it. "Working with what you're given" isn't always a negative thing.

[He smiles.]

Kristo: You'll be fine, I know you will.

Eiko: But what about-

Kristo: Tilda feels the same way as me, I assure you. Even if it doesn't seem like it.

[He waves goodbye to the two as he finally leaves. He flicks the room light off on the way out. Hyland and Eiko are alone.]

[Silence.]

Eiko: …I guess that's that then.

Hyland: Seems like it.

Eiko: And you're in my arms.

[Silence.]

Eiko: …I love you, Connor.

Hyland: …I love you too, Krista.

[Eiko pulls Connor up and presses her nose into his shade.]

Eiko: You won't leave me again, will you?

[Hyland turns on his bulb. His light casts a shadow of the two against the wall. It is the only thing visible in the now-dark room.]

Hyland: No. Never.
