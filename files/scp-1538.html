SCP-1538

Item #: SCP-1538

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: All instances of SCP-1538 are to be stored together in a standard storage area. SCP-1538 instances are to be categorised according to their sub-designations. Instances of SCP-1538-30, -37, -41 and -45 are not to be tested under any circumstances. All instances of SCP-1538 outside of containment are to be obtained under pretence of a stock recall.

Broadcasts of SCP-1538-B are to be blocked from public viewing and recorded for future reference. SCP-1538-B transmissions are to be treated as lethal auditory/visual cognitohazards until determined otherwise.

Description: SCP-1538 is a brand of bottled body wash soap marketed as “FACTORY BRAND ODOR REPLACING BODY WASH”. All individual instances of SCP-1538 are uniformly orange in colour and display the brand name, instance title and ingredient list, appearing to have these details stencilled onto the bottles post-manufacture. These bottles do not display any anomalous properties. SCP-1538 instances will always be encountered on appropriate shelving in various convenience stores; store records will not list any shipments of SCP-1538 being expected or received.

When an individual uses the contents of an SCP-1538 instance to bathe, the individual’s scent will change for a period of 24 hours to a predetermined scent dependent upon the title listed upon the SCP-1538 instance. The scent induced by an SCP-1538 instance can only be hindered or masked by the application of a different SCP-1538 instance’s contents – until the conclusion of the 24 hour period, the induced scent will be predominant over all other applied scents. The majority of SCP-1538 instances cause additional anomalous effects that persist as long as the predefined scent of the respective SCP-1538 instance does, after which all changes induced are typically reverted.

SCP-1538-B are a series of televised transmissions serving as advertisements for SCP-1538 instances. SCP-1538-B transmissions frequently occur during periods of time when a larger audience will be exposed to them and universally occur in countries where SCP-1538 instances can be encountered. The majority of SCP-1538-B transmissions display anomalous content and several broadcasts recorded are cognitohazaradous.

Addendum 1: As of ██/██/████, the majority of SCP-1538-B transmissions have become focused on promoting instances of SCP-1538-30 and SCP-1538-323, with the content of such transmissions frequently suggesting a rivalry between the two.

Addendum 2: On ██/██/████ a previously unseen SCP-1538-B broadcast was unsuccessfully intercepted. The SCP-1538-B transmission persisted for fifteen seconds and consisted of a human male promoting several new SCP-1538 instances. To date, none of the listed instances have been encountered.
