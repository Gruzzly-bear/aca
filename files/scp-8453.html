SCP-8453

Item #: SCP-8453

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8453 is currently pending official classification and containment. Until then, SCP-8453 is monitored, when possible, by Junior Researcher Anthony Davids.

Description: SCP-8453 is an inaccessible maintenance corridor behind J. Researcher Davids' office in Area-34. The corridor does not appear on any current or archived floor plans of Area-34. A sealed hatch, welded shut and concealed behind drywall, leads into the space.

SCP-8453 was discovered by J. Researcher Davids on April 15th, 2023. Loud thudding noises were heard behind a shelf, the removal of which uncovered the hatch.

Investigation with a handheld thermal imaging device indicated a temperature behind the hatch of 35-40°C. Attempts to open the hatch using cutting tools and drills have all failed.

On April 28th, J. Researcher Davids placed a microphone near the hatch. Over the following week, thudding and faint voices were recorded.

Audio Log 8453-1: April 28th, 2023

Voice: [muffled] …—me.

Davids: Huh, is someone there?

[Silence.]

Davids: Hello?

Voice: …worse than we thought.

Davids: What was worse?

Voice: Requesting… —ediate evac. This is Doct—… [static]…—ject Silhouette.

Davids: D-doctor? Who are you?

Audio Log 8453-4: May 1st, 2023

Voice: [static]…—amma 27, confirming…

[Gunfire.]

Davids: Whoa! What's happening?

Voice: ….—st visuals, requesting backup!

[Static, gunfire abruptly stops.]

Audio Log 8453-6: May 4th, 2023

Voice 1: [static]…—imicry was a mistake! Look around you!

Voice 2: If only we had… [static] …—and this is what we get.

Davids: There's two of you now! Hello?

Voice 1: Whatever, we just need to—…

[Static]

Audio Log 8453-7: May 5th, 2023

Voice: —off, completely.

[Loud, mechanical noises.]

Voice: I don't want any signs of—….

[Mechanical noises grow louder, accompanied by the sound of crushing concrete, deafening the voice.]

Audio Log 8453-8: May 6th, 2023

Voice: [Static]…—ing left. Only… —uck and bodies.

[Wet, crunching noises.]

Voice: …can be of some use…

Voice: [muffled] …—me.

Two incident reports were submitted by J. Researcher Davids, both of which have been declined.

Personal Log: Anthony Davids, May 13th 2023

I've been recording the hatch for a while now. I think it just repeats after a week? At least the voices do.

I was up late last night working on another SCP I’m actually assigned to. And… You know that feeling when you know someone is watching you? That's what I was getting from the hatch. And, when I got close to it… I could've sworn I heard sobbing. I checked the recording afterward— nothing. But it was on. I know it was on.


Anyway, I searched up 'Silhouette,' since it kept coming up. There was an old Project here in -34 named that, but I can't find anything about it. Who led it, what it was about, nothing. All I found was an empty directory made in 1991.














Note from Site Director Ezra Everest

Following the discovery of the above draft in the 8453 slot, Anthony Davids has been assigned to therapy and relieved of duty. Clearly, he's been having a hard time adjusting to this new line of work, and could use a well-deserved break.

He has also been reprimanded for the misuse of Foundation equipment.
