SCP-3084-A

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3084-A is to be held in a standard humanoid containment cell in Site-120's isolation ward. Its cell is to be insulated with no less than 2 meters of antithaumic spellophane and lined with 5 cm thick scrantonite alloy plates. All materials used in the construction of the chamber must first be consecrated by a major archbishop of the Catholic Church, a patriarch of the Cogwork Orthodox Church, a karcist of the Nälkä faith, a high warlock of the United Church of Satan, Scientist, and a finger-saint of the Fifthist Collective, then unblessed by an antipriest of SAPPHIRE.

Testing with SCP-3084 requires the approval of Dr. Daniel Asheworth and two other members of Site-120's Director Council. Objects with a conceptual volatility rating of 3 or higher may not be brought into SCP-3084-A's cell. Thaumaturgy may not be performed inside SCP-3084-A's cell. SCP-3084-A may request access to entertainment media or other accommodations within conceptual volatility guidelines. Personnel may not enter its cell without an esoteric materials suit that has been fitted with high-grade auricular and visual thaumohazard filters.

All SCP-3084-B instances are to be recovered from GoI-156 ("Ralliston & Savage").Ralliston & Savage: Warsaw-based thaumaturgic finance firm formerly contracted by GoI-012 to manage SCP-3084. offices and stored in Site-120's morgue, pending further examination. The offices themselves are to undergo thorough esoteric reduction treatments by MTF Gamma-21 ("Master Abaters") in collaboration with Area-21 specialists.

SCP-3084 must not be allowed to lose its metastability..Hiemal: A system of 2+ anomalies that keep one another in a state of equilibrium.

Description: SCP-3084 is the collective designation for the 62,748,517 distinguishable curses, hexes, jinxes, and anathemas created by GoI-012 ("Marshall, Carter, and Dark")'s business practices, either as a natural consequence of their actions, or having been purposefully cultivated for the sake of profit. Prior to Incident 3084-1, GoI-012 appears to have prevented SCP-3084 from impeding their day-to-day operations by embedding it in the sub-Veil stock market, containing its effects through thaumaturgic stock trading.

Following Incident 3084-1, the anomaly has been solely tethered to [DATA EXORCISED], a 32-year-old stock trader (designated SCP-3084-A) formerly in the employ of GoI-156. Though the cumulative effects of SCP-3084 are more than sufficient to completely obliterate any individual human, they appear to have reached a state of relative maledictive metastability, allowing SCP-3084-A to survive with only 89% of his body being [DATA EXORCISED].

Though SCP-3084-A has been largely spared the curse complex's influence, this has not made SCP-3084 any less dangerous. Directly hearing or seeing SCP-3084-A in-person without sufficient protection will almost certainly cause fatal harm to the observer. Additionally, SCP-3084-A's name, physical status, financial status, and marital status have become Class-C infomaledictive thaumohazards, being potential vectors of curse transmittance to those of above-average net worth.

In the event that the anomaly loses its metastability, SCP-3084 will immediately enter an imprecation cascade, terminating SCP-3084-A and every person he's ever come into physical or verbal contact with (barring individuals that are heavily shielded from thaumaturgical harm).

Addendum 3084-1: Incident 3084-1

On 24/10/2008, [DATA EXPUNGED] received a call on their personal cell phone from PoI-012-01 ("Percival Darke") regarding SCP-3084.

PoI-012-01: It's me. I know you requested that I not use this number for business, but I've just watched some of my best contractors turn inside out and truly cannot be bothered.

So. We've had something of an incident at the Ralliston & Savage offices. It seems their top bean counter made a series of anathemathematical blunders and royally [DATA EXORCISED] himself, along with our entire curse trading enterprise. Dumped the lot of it on his own head.

Did you already know about the curse trading? I suppose the cat's out of the bag now… Gods below, it's going to take an age and half to dry-clean this suit.

You'll be doing us something of a favor, so I'll handle the cover-up — just make sure all the bodies are cleaned up by next week, yes? Do take care, though, the building must be seeped through with centuries of malignant magic by now.

Oh, and if that [DATA EXORCISED] survives, tell him I'll string him up by his entrails if he so much as steps foot in one of my buildings again. Ta.

Foundation personnel arrived at GoI-156 offices to discover that every person in the building, save for an unconscious SCP-3084-A, had died in a different way. Currently, only three of the GoI-156 corpses (collectively designated SCP-3084-B) have yet been autopsied. Results are as follows:

  1. Urszula Ryba, Accountant. Appears to have simultaneously burned and froze to death. Blood transmuted into liquid pyrite..Otherwise known as "fool's gold."
  2. Kazimierz Brzezicki, Intern. Death from severed cervical vertebrae. Chest cavity filled with unidentified human heads at 1/4 scale.
  3. Manfred Jedynak, Chief Executive Officer. [DATA EXORCISED]. Body identified through advanced psychometry.

GoI-156 office interior. SCP-3084-B instance in frame.

Addendum 3084-2: Interview Log

<BEGIN LOG>

<SCP-3084-A is sitting on the floor next to its cot, staring at its arms.>

Dr. Asheworth: <Communicating through an interior speaker.> [DATA EXORCISED]? Can you hear me?

<SCP-3084-A staggers to its feet, looking at the ceiling.>

SCP-3084-A: Y-yes. I can hear you. Where am I? Is this a hospital?

Dr. Asheworth: My name is Dr. Asheworth, I'm with the Foundation. Do you know what that is?

SCP-3084-A: I think so. You keep magic a secret, right?

Dr. Asheworth: More or less. Do you know why you're here?

SCP-3084-A: I mean, I'm cursed, right? I don't really remember what happened yesterday, but working with curses is my job, so…

Dr. Asheworth: In a word, yes. You have been cursed.

SCP-3084-A: What cursed me? Don't think I've ever seen one that makes you [DATA EXORCISED].

Dr. Asheworth: I'm afraid you've been cursed by, well… everything.

SCP-3084-A: Everything?

Dr. Asheworth: Everything.

SCP-3084-A: Name one.

Dr. Asheworth: You've been cursed by the Devil, for starters.

SCP-3084-A: [DATA EXORCISED]. What about God?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Which one?

Dr. Asheworth: Every deity we have on record, plus a few new ones we've discovered through you.

SCP-3084-A: Have I been cursed by angry spirits?

Dr. Asheworth: In great numbers, yes.

SCP-3084-A: Ghosts?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Faeries?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Witches?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Vampires?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: The Antichrist?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Those chain emails that tell you to forward them to ten of your—

Dr. Asheworth: Yes, you've been cursed by a few of those as well.

SCP-3084-A: What about tulpas?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Cryptids?

Dr. Asheworth: Yes.

SCP-3084-A: Mermaids?

Dr. Asheworth: A little bit, yes.

SCP-3084-A: Mermaids are real?!

Dr. Asheworth: Why is that the one you're— I think you're focusing on the wrong thing, here.

SCP-3084-A: [DATA EXORCISED]… How am I still alive? I mean, I feel fine. Felt fine. I think I'm feeling a panic attack right now. Is that normal, should I be feeling normal?!

Dr. Asheworth: Please try to remain calm. Currently, all of your curses appear to have reached a state of equilibrium, cancelling one another out.

SCP-3084-A: Good, good. I mean, that's good, right? Can you get rid of them?

Dr. Asheworth: We're… not certain, at the moment. If we try to remove them one at a time, then each thread we pull will almost certainly set the rest of them off. It would take decades of thaumaturgic simulations to figure out how to defuse a maledictive network of this size without serious injury.

SCP-3084-A: Why not remove them all at once? Is that possible?

Dr. Asheworth: We do possess methods of bulk hex removal, but the thaumaturgic backlash from removing that many curses simultaneously would probably atomize you.

SCP-3084-A: [DATA EXORCISED]!

Dr. Asheworth: I understand that you're upset, but I'd advise against swearing for the time being.

SCP-3084-A: Seriously?! I'm a ticking—[EXORCISED]—time bomb and you're thinking about whether I'm cur— Oh. Right, no, yeah, I'll hold off.

<END LOG>

AFTERWORD: Approximately two weeks after SCP-3084-A entered custody, a velvet-lined box full of nonanomalous ashes manifested within SCP-3084-A's containment cell. After determining that the sudden anomalous activity hadn't disrupted SCP-3084's metastability, the box was removed from the cell.

Forensic analysis has since ascertained that the ashes were taken from the ruins of SCP-3084-A's house, which burned down a week prior. When questioned, SCP-3084-A described the box as his "severance pay."
