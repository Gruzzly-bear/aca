SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES:

SCP-9950 is Aizsaule1 class.

Due to the sheer ideatic density2 of SCP-9950, full separation of its various related memeplexes from baseline reality, and thus proper containment, is infeasible. Current research is centered on the manufacturing of a hypothetical narritive space without the existence of SCP-9950, regardless of the logical impossibility of doing so within an SCP-9950-infected plane.

DESCRIPTION:

SCP-9950 is a possibly-sentient theoretical and metaphysical parasite currently inhabiting all planes of conceptualization known to humanity.

SCP-9950’s anomalous properties have yet to be fully proven, however it has been provided an SCP designation due to the sheer improbability of its prevalence across planes which should not possess its ideatic characteristics, as well as recovered information from the Huǒjù zhi Zi occult organization3, which suggests to a point in history without the existence of SCP-9950.

The true physical parameters of SCP-9950 cannot be determined due to the subjective nature of most of its manifestations.

ADDENDUM-1:

The following is a comprehensive list of the planes of conceptualization SCP-9950 has affected, and the ideatic density of SCP-9950 within said planes.

INFOSPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  INTANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 100ρω

DEFINITION

The collective metaspace of all possible ideas

EFFECTS

Due to the nature of the Infosphere SCP-9950, while fully present in its entirety within it, has not been observed to have a structural impact on it as a whole.

PATASPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  INTANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 46ρω

DEFINITION

The collective metaspace of all possible narratives.

EFFECTS

As stated above, 100% of narratives known to the Foundation have been infected by SCP-9950 in some form. SCP-9950 most commonly manifests within the Patasphere as either an external or internal antagonistic force, regardless of genre. However various culturally significant narratives within younger genre demographics have been altered to include aspects of SCP-9950 The rate of these alterations have been shown to increase during periods of societal destabilization attributed to ecological crisis.

NOÖSPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  INTANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 34ρω

DEFINITION

The collective metaspace of the human unconscious.

EFFECTS

While no metastructual damage to the noösphere cashed by SCP-9950 has been observed, the number of thought energy condensing around the "hexagonal" and the "color" nodes have increased, by proxy increasing essophysical manifestation events correlating to aspects of SCP-9950

Recent noöspheric examination of unconscious anti-concepts have revealed a continuous merging of SCP-9950 with certain "Primal fears" experienced by the human population. Current estimates date the beginning of this merging to have begun around 300,000 years ago.

AKIVASPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  SEMI-TANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 31ρω

DEFINITION

The metaphysical membrane encompassing interactions between belief and reality.

EFFECTS

Although average ideatic density is higher overall within the Akivasphere, no notable effects have been observed aside from minor semiohazardous alterations to significant religious texts & timezones for certain holidays. Past manifestations of Apex-tier pluripotent entities releted to SCP-9950 have largely decreased due to their theological irrelevance.

ASTEROSPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  SEMI-TANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 28ρω

DEFINITION

The collective metaspace of human dreams & Thaumaturgy.

EFFECTS

Despite of the relatively low ideatic density related to SCP-9950 the meta structure of the Asterosphere has been greatly impacted, in what is assumed to be a parasymbiotic pact throughout the entire astral plane. This has led to a decrease in communications between planes, leading the Foundation to be live in the lessening of biodiversity within the astral plane.

Furthermore, unlike all expected thaumaturgical backlash events within workings4 thaumaturgical workings dependent on SCP-9950 reactions propagate further essophysical manifestations of SCP-9950 during backlash events.

ONTOSPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  TANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 25ρω

DEFINITION

Physical baseline reality, encompassing all natural laws.

EFFECTS

SCP-9950 has irreparably imbedded itself within all laws of motion, chemistry & thermodynamics.

Reverse engineering the state of the ontosphere before the appearance of SCP-9950 has been deemed impossible.

CYBERSPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  TANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 06ρω

DEFINITION

Collective Metaspace of all digital data.

EFFECTS

No significant traces of SCP-9950 affliction observed within the cybersphere besides minor cultural shifts observed in the internet & the behavioral programming of certain AIC’s. This is assumed to be caused by the low ideatic compatibility SCP-9950 possesses with the cybersphere.

BIOSPHERE
—

PLANE VISIBILITY:  TANGIBLE

IDEATIC DENSITY: 12ρω

DEFINITION

The collective metaspace of all biological information.

EFFECTS

The low amount of SCP-9950 related ideatic density present within earths biosphere has been attributed to the high amount of conceptual intersections between the thoughts of sentient life and SCP-9950.

All Flora & Fauna presents on earth have developed biological reactions in response to SCP-9950 throughout history. Due to the complex nature of their conceptualization humans have also been observed engaging in mental reactive mechanisms adapted to and or altered by SCP-9950 such as cultural shifts in food preservation, food gathering, clothing preferences & worship of memeplexes antithetical to SCP-9950.







ADDENDUM-2:

On July 3rd 2034, the largest essophysical manifestation of SCP-9950 was recorded and designated SCP-9950-1. Records of SCP-9950’s existence have been found dating back roughly 34 million years ago with various proven historical records. Considering its area of 14,200,000 km², the number of entities, settlements and landmarks found within it, the appearance of SCP-9950-1 has been declared largest semiohazardous event in history.

Due to the newly confirmed existence of SCP-9950 & SCP-9950-1, no containment, neutralization or planetary evacuation efforts are being considered at this time. Reclassification to -EX is pending.
