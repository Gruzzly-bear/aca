SCP-9101

Item #: SCP-9101
keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9101 has been placed in standard humanoid containment. Additional security measures should be taken if needed.

Updated Containment Procedures: SCP-9101's containment security has been upgraded. Increased observation is recommended due to the nature of its ability.

Updated Containment Procedures: In addition to standard containment protocols, SCP-9101's restraints should be checked for defects or signs of tampering at irregular intervals throughout the day.

Updated Containment Procedures: SCP-9101's current state of unconsciousness is to be maintained indefinitely through intravenous anesthetics.

Updated Containment Procedures: SCP-9101 is to receive surreptitious oxytocin therapy several times a day, always in close proximity to Junior Researcher Margaret Moriyya.

Updated Containment Procedures: SCP-9101 is to receive narcotic pain relief every 12 hours. SCP-9101's nerve block catheter should also be kept fully supplied with non-opioid numbing medication.

Updated Containment Procedures: SCP-9101's body should be regularly monitored for anomalous activity.

Updated Containment Procedures: Attempts at containment are ongoing. See Addendum 9101.3.

Description: SCP-9101 is Edmund Watts, a 52-year-old repairman who is apparently incapable of remaining within any dynamic for an extended period of time. This effect extends to specific locations, relationships, and states of being. While SCP-9101 is capable of using its ability directly, the effect may also manifest when SCP-9101 is unconscious, distracted, or otherwise unable to provide direct input.1

Addendum 9101.1: Containment Attempts and Testing
Due to the nature of SCP-9101's ability, containment and testing are considered to be simultaneously performed.

Attempt Result Notes
Standard Euclid humanoid containment Containment failure During a rare lapse in observation, SCP-9101 simply walked out of its cell. It was recovered in the site cafeteria while eating a bagel.
Standard Keter humanoid containment Containment failure SCP-9101 once again walked out of its cell despite continuous observation. It was immediately recovered and returned to containment without incident.
Physical restraints in addition to standard containment Containment failure SCP-9101 was recovered outside containment without restraints. When asked, it claimed that they were "defective" and had "just fallen off".
Chemical restraints in addition to standard containment Containment failure SCP-9101 was found outside the door to its cell, still unable to move. It regained motor functions marginally faster than expected.
Psychological manipulation Containment failure Despite an intensive hypnotic and chemical campaign to convince SCP-9101 that it had fallen in love with its head researcher, it managed to leave the facility and was recovered while trying to hitchhike on a service road. Previous assessments clearly underestimated its mental fortitude.
Amputation Containment failure Due to an administrative error with SCP-9101's medication regimen, the amputated limb was partially regenerated.
Neutralization2 Containment failure SCP-9101 regained consciousness through unknown means several days later. Psychiatric counseling was offered and rejected in no uncertain terms.
Adaptive Containment Protocol Ongoing See below.

Addendum 9101.2: The Adaptive Containment Protocol

In an effort to reduce financial and personnel costs associated with containing particularly difficult anomalies, the Foundation has authorized the use of the Adaptive Containment Protocol, a dimensionally-agnostic thaumaturgical system with three primary goals:

1. Design a structural system that can contain the target anomaly
2. Construct and maintain said system
3. Identify and record the target anomaly's pattern of attempted breaches to simplify further containment

While the overarching ACP system draws from the same codebase and power source for each assignment, every anomaly is assigned its own Adaptive Containment Entity to personalize its containment. Each resulting ACE's database is quarantined to prevent ideological or cognitohazardous contamination in either direction.

The specific ACP assignment containing SCP-9101 was initiated on September 9th, 2025.
