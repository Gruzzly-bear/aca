Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-8431 is currently uncontained, but poses no danger to Foundation operations. SCP-8431-A is to be held in a standard anomalous item locker in Site-210, which is to be checked on a weekly basis for any new communication being relayed from SCP-8431.

Description:

SCP-8431 is a Golden Retriever possessing cybernetic enhancements with the capability to translate its thoughts into ASCII text data, which can then be transferred to SCP-8431-A through an unknown process. This is achieved through a program constructed in Python, though the copy obtained by Foundation agents seems to lack any anomalous aspects, and thus functionality. The enhancements are centred around SCP-8431's shoulders, and cause difficulty walking for extended periods of time. Messages from SCP-8431 do not exceed 256 characters, due to aforementioned technical limitations. Additionally, SCP-8431 is capable of entering the noosphere1 as a physical location, though it is yet to be determined whether return to baseline reality is possible.

SCP-8431-A is a computer terminal with no visible identification, which receives all communication from SCP-8431. Subsequent to the receival of a message, a previously concealed compartment in the base of SCP-8431-A will open, revealing a USB port, which allows for the sending of messages to SCP-8431. It would appear there is a 24 hour delay between initial message sending and receiving. The object was originally obtained via a raid on the Chaos Insurgency.

Addendum 8431-a:

Date of Log: 03/01/2020 - 08/01/2020
Receiver: Dr South
Location: Secrecy

| USER-CANINE-10
Insurgency? Insurgency?

| USER-RECEIVER
This is not the Insurgency.

| USER-CANINE-10
Who is this?

| USER-RECEIVER
I am Dr South. I work for the SCP Foundation.

| USER-CANINE-10
South? South! You have come for me!

| USER-RECEIVER
Please identify yourself.

| USER-CANINE-10
You know me, South. I know you, I love you. I am your dog, May.

Subsequent to these messages, partially intact schematics for SCP-8431's cybernetic components were recovered from the original site of SCP-8431-A's discovery, along with a preserved section of fur belonging to a Golden Retriever, which is believed to originate from SCP-8431. Dr South was made aware of this following a brief inquiry.

Addendum 8431-b:

Date of Log: 10/01/2020 - 16/01/2020
Receiver: Dr South
Location: Secrecy

| USER-RECEIVER
May. Would you prefer I use that name in future conversation?

| USER-CANINE-10
What else would you call me?

| USER-RECEIVER
Understood. May, according to information we have received, you are located in the noosphere, which is not a physical location. How has this been achieved?

| USER-CANINE-10
It took them several attempts. There were nine dogs before me. I was the success. They kept stabbing and cutting at me, and then I fell asleep. When I woke up, there was a weight on my shoulders. I think that's why I'm here.

| USER-RECEIVER
Thank you, May. Do you know where you are within the noosphere?

| USER-CANINE-10
They called it Secrets.

| USER-RECEIVER
Alright. Please could you continue onward. We would appreciate any information you have on the geography of the noosphere.

Addendum 8431-c:

Date of Log: 23/01/2020 - 27/01/2020
Receiver: Dr South
Location: Death

| USER-CANINE-10
It's dark here. Very dark. I smell flesh. I don't like it here. It feels like an ending.

| USER-RECEIVER

| USER-CANINE-10

Where next? I don't want to stay here long.

| USER-RECEIVER
Onward, May. Onward.

Addendum 8431-d:

Subsequent to the disappearance of Dr South, Junior Researcher Shelian was permitted to take his place in conversing with SCP-8431, under the pretence of being Dr South.

Date of Log: 10/02/2020 - 15/02/2020
Receiver: Junior Researcher Shelian
Location: Connection / Belief

| USER-CANINE-10
I made it, South. It's happy here, like an electric happiness. But my paws won't stop hurting. Can I rest here?

| USER-RECEIVER
May, can you describe where you are?

| USER-CANINE-10
It's warm. Reminds me of when we used to play, at the park. And you would throw the ball, and I would run as fast as I could, to catch it, so you didn't lose it, and so you could keep it and so that you could be proud of me, and throw it again, as I did so2

| USER-RECEIVER
Thank you, SCP-8431. You may continue onward.

| USER-CANINE-10
What do those numbers mean?

| USER-RECEIVER
Nothing.

Junior Researcher Shelian was later reprimanded for using SCP-8431's formal designation, an act which could decrease cooperation by the anomaly.

Date of Log: 29/02/2020 - 03/03/2020

Location: Names / Designation

| USER-CANINE-10

| USER-RECEIVER

It is undetermined whether SCP-8431 believed the last message in the above log. However, SCP-8431 remained in communication with Junior Shelian. From this point onward, journeys made by SCP-8431 begin to increase in duration, likely due to issues in health and mobility.

Addendum 8431-h:

Prior to this log, a council was held by the respective Heads of Departments at Site-210, concerning a proposed increase in security measures to avoid intrusion into the SCP-8431 project. However, due to SCP-8431's irreparable decline in health, it was agreed to maintain security at the current level.

Date of Log: 27/04/2020 - 02/05/2020
Receiver: Junior Researcher Shelian
Location: Depression

| USER-CANINE-10
I can't do it. It hurts so badly. I can't move without it hurting.

| USER-RECEIVER
SCP-8431, can you describe your current surroundings?

| USER-CANINE-10
The same, but more. Like the pain. It just keeps getting worse.

| USER-RECEIVER
SCP-8431, are you still capable of moving onward?

| USER-CANINE-10
Yes, Foundation. Please don't make me. I just want to rest.

| USER-RECEIVER
Continue onward. This is an order.
