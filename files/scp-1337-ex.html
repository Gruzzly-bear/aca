Special Containment Procedures

Obsolete.

Description:

SCP-1337-EX were nonanomalous non-fungible tokens created and sold by Vikander-Kneed Technical Media. SCP-1337-EX incorporated anomalous knowledge possessed by Vikander-Kneed, but were not anomalous in themselves.

As of 3/5/2021, it is believed no further instances of SCP-1337-EX will be created.

Descriptions of non-fungible tokens are widely available through civilian sources.

Addendum 1:

The following communication was sent between Drs. Lillihammer and Yang on 3/10/2021.

To: Ezekiel Yang, PhD, Financial Esoterica
From: Lillian Lillihammer, PhD, Site-43

Subject: FWD: Product Discontinuation
Date: 10/03/2021

Ezekiel,

People are shit.

To: Lillian Lillihammer, PhD, Site-43
From: Vikander-Kneed Public Outreach

Subject: Product Discontinuation
Date: 10/03/2021

Dear Lillian,

We're very grateful of your organization's prolific patronage of our limited edition one-of-a-kind VKTM NFTs! Unfortunately, due to an unprecedented and unpredictable increase of competition in the NFT space, we will be discontinuing this line of products. We don't have the star power of Banksy, after all.

Your previous NFTs are worth exactly what you can sell them for!

From,
Your friends at Vikander-Kneed Technical Media.
