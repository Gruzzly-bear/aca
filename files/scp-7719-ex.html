SCP-7719-EX

Hallway of Floor B2

Special Containment Procedures will be updated if containment challenges arise.

Description

SCP-7719 is SCP Foundation Site+19.Unknown relation to Site-19., an underground facility of unknown origin and location. Architecture and interior decoration are in line with Department of Design's parameters for compact sites, save the substandard lighting system.

SCP-7719 continually expands at an indeterminate rate. As of writing, SCP-7719 has four underground floors.

Addendum 7719.1

Each floor contains from two to four rooms of various utilities. The following is a list of the rooms on each floor and description thereof, as well as its contents, if possible.

Floor B4

Room Two

A standard Foundation conference room. Several bloody right handprints are strewn across the walls. All members of the Overseer Council (excluding O5-13) are seated in the room.

Room One

A standard Foundation conference room. Shattered pieces of metallic and red crystalline materials are laid on the table.

Floor B3

Room Three

A large storage room.Room size noticeably exceeds external physical dimensions. containing various miscellaneous items, such as a bronze humanoid statue, several pink plastic lawn flamingos, an oily mass of flesh, among others.

Room Two

A manager office room belonging to Ronald Stimson, as written on the name plate. There is no indication of the office ever having been occupied or used.

Room One

An empty, charred room. Entering it inflicts a slight feeling of wanderlust.

Floor B2

All four rooms are densely packed with coffins.Faint smells of sulfur are noted. and can not be entered. Signs on the doors read "Disposal Joint currently at capacity. Refer to Mount Qomolangma Long-term Storage Facility."

Floor B1

Room Two
A display room, showcasing a scale model of Prototype Site-19/█████████ ████████████████ Research Facility. Several cabinets line the far wall, containing empty casefiles belonging to "Lieutenant M."

Room One

A studio filled with various supplies and miscellaneous items, such as spray paint, concrete mix, blueprints and blank HMCL ID cards. At the center of the room rests a blank canvas with the word "SORRY" written on it.

Ground Floor

The ground floor comprises a long tunnel, connecting the facility's entrance to the stairs leading below. The pipes and wirings on the overhang are affixed with small, metallic dodecahedron shells.

Ground floor intake tunnel. The entrance leads outside.

The entrance lacks any identifiable markings barring a small metal plaque above the doorway, unreadable due to wear and tarnish.

Image unavailable.

Object Class Explained

Special Containment Procedures

The entrance to SCP-7719-EX has been padlocked, and is to be patrolled by a single guard. Inside access is forbidden.

Description

SCP-7719-EX is an abandoned Foundation subterranean vault in Northeast Greenland National Park, formerly Long-term Storage Site-19.

Improper ID designation.Likely due to typo. led to it not being properly indexed by the database, leading to clerical errors and eventual cut-off from Foundation supply line.

SCP-7719-EX's last-known inventory check occurred on May 13th, ████.
