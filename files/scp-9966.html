Automated Real-time Star Hiding

Public-facing images of the night sky are altered before release. Disinformation campaigns are utilized to reinforce the belief that any discrepancies between amateur photographs and official star maps are due to camera limitations, atmospheric distortion, and inexperience from the photographer.

Civilian and academic observatories are to remain under continuous Foundation oversight through agents embedded in national space agencies, academic institutions, and organizations performing astrometric surveys. All digital telescope software/firmware distributed to the public must include A.R.S.H. programming that automatically distorts stellar positions of celestial bodies by >10 arcseconds.

DESCRIPTION: SCP-9966 is the night sky as observed from the surface of Earth. When instances of SCP-9966 digital or physical are viewed with the unaided human eye or through unaltered optical instruments located on Earth’s surface, or a dataset bearing the signature of SCP-9966 is read by a sufficiently sentient artificial intelligence model, the arrangement of stellar bodies constitutes a memetic phenomenon. This effect is not present when the same pattern is observed from outside Earth’s atmosphere.

Exposure to the unfiltered SCP-9966 pattern induces a compulsive drive to ascend vertically. Symptom onset occurs within 2–5 seconds of exposure and follows a consistent sequence:

0–2 seconds: Subject reports intense cognitive engagement characterized by sensations of comprehension and significance.

2–3 seconds: Subject enters a catatonic state. Voluntary motor function ceases. Pupillary dilation exceeds normal physiological limits, frequently resulting in ocular damage.

3–5 seconds: Subject regains motor activity and demonstrates singular behavioral fixation on vertical ascent. Movement is directed upward by any available means, including but not limited to climbing, scaling structures, or attempting to exit through elevated apertures.

Unrestrained subjects will continue ascent attempts until physical incapacity, external intervention, or amnestic treatment prevents further activity.

ADDENDUM 9966.1: DISCOVERY

The nature of SCP-9966 was first identified following the “Blackout Event” in the town of ███████, Alaska on 20/11/1985. A region-wide power failure coincided with clear weather conditions. Foundation satellites monitoring for anomalous events detected a complete cessation of electromagnetic emissions from the town.

A recovery team discovered the entire civilian population deceased. Cadavers were located primarily on streets and yards adjacent to residential structures, with injuries consistent with repeated high-velocity impact from elevated positions. Multiple bloodstains, footprints, and drag marks were identified on rooftops, indicating subjects ascended and leapt multiple times until fatal trauma occurred.

The absence of any conventional explanatory factor, combined with satellite confirmation of unobstructed visibility of the night sky, led to the initial containment hypothesis.

SUB-PROTOCOL OUTLINE

PURPOSE: Establish global countermeasures in the event of uncontrolled, worldwide exposure to SCP-9966.

ABSTRACT: SCP-9966’s anomalous visual properties present the potential for irreversible EK-Class (“Eclipse”) scenario development. Preventative measures must therefore focus on ensuring the elimination of human capacity for visual reception of SCP-9966’s stellar pattern. Protocol-NOCTIS authorizes global dissemination of ophthalmological compounds capable of inducing permanent, irreversible retinal degradation, causing functional blindness.

METHOD

SECTION A: Dissemination Vector
Distribution of Class-Ω ophthalmological compounds via international food and water supply chains. Secondary dissemination procedures (aerosol agent dispersal, agricultural treatment, and livestock inoculation) are authorized if initial procedures prove insufficient.

SECTION B: Retinal Collapse Induction
Opthalmological compounds are engineered to chemically degrade retinal tissue beyond repair, resulting in functional blindness within 24–72 hours of ingestion. Projected efficacy is estimated at 92–96% of the global population, with remaining individuals subject to targeted interventions.

SECTION C: Civilian Management
Collapse of civilian infrastructure and population post-NOCTIS is anticipated. The Protocol explicitly deprioritizes preservation of civilian populations, directing any and all available resources toward continuity of Foundation control, containment operations, and maintenance of post-NOCTIS civilian surveillance.

SECTION D: Escalation to Eclipse Scenario
Should NOCTIS dissemination fail to achieve sufficient reduction in SCP-9966 exposure, surviving Foundation personnel are to transition to EK-Class “Eclipse” scenario procedures. Priority is given to:

  • Preservation of informational continuity (physical and digital archives, mnemonic redundancy).
  • Maintenance of long-term containment and monitoring capacity.
  • Termination of all efforts to control non-essential civilian populations to prevent further exposure cascades.

UPDATE: In accordance with O5 directive, all materials and deployment infrastructure necessary for NOCTIS implementation are to be maintained at operational standby. Effectiveness is contingent on immediate initiation at the first sign of global-scale SCP-9966 breach.."Overkill."

PROCEDURES:.Pausa class anomalies no longer display anomalous properties, but may do so again in the future. .Inimical class anomalies must be decommissioned, as containment efforts only worsen their effects. The existence of sapient cognition is the vector of SCP-9966 hostility. Containment is no longer achievable through external suppression. Only the elimination of human sapience can reduce further hostile activity.

ANTITHINK machines are to be deployed globally. All surviving civilians, and all remaining Foundation personnel, are to undergo compulsory induction into ANTITHINK conversion.

Archival records, digital or physical, containing references to:

  • neutrino-cognitron entanglement
  • extraterrestrial religious scriptures
  • the concept of cognitohazards

are to be destroyed in their entirety.

This document constitutes the final containment directive. Upon successful induction of global ANTITHINK conversion, SCP-9966 will have no subject of engagement. Containment is achieved only through the termination of thought.

DESCRIPTION: SCP-9966 is a cognitively-hostile phenomenon expressed through neutrino-cognitron entanglement.

Any neutrino emission originating external to Earth’s cognitosphere transmits SCP-9966’s data signature. Exposure to this data by a sapient cognitive circuitry results in immediate cognitive termination, resulting in extreme damage to the cognitive circuitry.

Analysis concludes that SCP-9966 targets sapient cognition exclusively. Non-sapient organisms, mechanical systems, and post-ANTITHINK subjects remain unaffected.

All prior containment measures (A.R.S.H. suppression, optical shielding, Protocol NOCTIS) are classified as ineffective. The only functional countermeasure is compulsory ANTITHINK conversion to remove sapience as a valid host state.

ADDENDUM 9966.2: PROTOCOL-ANTITHINK

PROTOCOL-ANTITHINK

DECOMMISSION PROTOCOL #9966

Dir. Calvin Bold
Department of Decommissioning

PROTOCOL OUTLINE

PURPOSE: Decommission SCP-9966 through induced regression of human sapience, creating a cognitively inert successor population resistant to SCP-9966.

ABSTRACT: SCP-9966’s neutrino-cognitron entanglement renders all sapient organisms terminally vulnerable. Standard containment has proven insufficient to mitigate global extinction risk. Protocol-ANTITHINK authorizes the deployment of Bold-Kelvin Auto-Reliant Artificial Sapience Cognitive Sink Domes. Colloquially called ANTITHINK machines, these employ a cognitron-drain programme designed to forcibly recess the cognitive capabilities of volunteer sapient entities, generating a population of pseudo-humans that are capable of potential sapience but incapable of actualizing it. This regression creates a low cognitive field, immune to the effects of SCP-9966, theoretically allowing long-term re-emergence of sapience and reformation of an uncontaminated Cognitosphere..Or, the crows beat us to it.

METHOD

SECTION A: Subject Induction
Cognizant entities designated for ANTITHINK inoculation will be secured within the ANTITHINK machine. Once activated, the apparatus will initiate controlled siphoning of the subject’s cognitive capabilities. This process is accelerated for civilians through cognitively taxing stimulus, provided under the guise of leisure activities. To ensure the continuity of Foundation oversight over ANTITHINK inoculation, all stimulus given to Foundation personnel are altered to redact redundant and irrelevant information to reduce cognitive load.

SECTION B: Cognitive Recession
Over the course of full drainage, the subject will undergo irreversible sapience recession, resulting in a sentient but non-sapient state. These entities designated Homo Sentiens retain baseline biological functionality while existing outside SCP-9966’s lethal cognitohazard parameters.

SECTION C: Negative Space Generation
The collective presence of H. Sentiens induces a large-scale cognitively “blank” substrate. This cognitron-negative environment prevents SCP-9966 from maintaining anomalous resonance, causing the complete dissolution of Earth’s cognitosphere, effectively neutralizing SCP-9966's influence.

SECTION D: Evolutionary Continuity
Foundation models predict that, given sufficient temporal scale, H. Sentiens populations may re-evolve into sapience under post-SCP-9966 conditions. Whether this outcome constitutes true "humanity" is formally deemed irrelevant under Decommission Directive 9966.
