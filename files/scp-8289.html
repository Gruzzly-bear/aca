SCP-8289

Item #: SCP-8289

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-8289 shall not be entered outside of testing. Site-70 administration has been instructed to never schedule meetings in SCP-8289 without authorization from the Indeterminates Research Group. In the event of a containment breach, enter SCP-8289 without scheduling a meeting.

Description: It is undefined behavior to reside within SCP-8289 outside of a scheduled meeting. SCP-8289 is formally designated Room-70-094-8289, a 12-person meeting room in Site-70. The SCP-8289 research staff has been optimized to an automated containment and calendar booking system.
