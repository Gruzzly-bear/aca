1.2 Initial Discovery and Progress Report

Following the approval for OPERATION BLANKSHOT, informant Executive 37Z detailed the location of Substance 55.03 and Substance 51.11. Located at Site-19 of SCPF International, initial assessments have ruled out any overt military action, as the facility is heavily fortified, armed, and within reach of other similar facilities of SCPF International. Several proposals are to be considered.

Proposal 1

Author: Major S. Anderson
Summary: A general assault at 2200 and 2400 hours. Current observations reveal these hours to be the least occupied, and an assault will allow a greater degree of freedom for assaulting troops. The general mitigation of harm to unarmed employees will earn goodwill in certain factions of the GOC and provide a semi-strong diplomatic cover against NAI 02.
Outline: An initial aerial assault, comprised of fast-moving F-15s, will draw SCPF aerial assets away from Site-19. A second fleet is to approach on the opposing side and provide ground cover, forcing armed SCPF assets to engage the fleet. A ground assault concentrated on the west and south sides will storm the facility to retrieve assets. Upon capture, all ground assets are to quickly withdraw and conceal themselves within the desert. Aerial assets will return to base.
DECISION: REJECTED
.

Proposal 2

Author: Lt. Colonel A. Shah
Summary: A simultaneous attack on multiple SCPF facilities, followed by a large-scale assault on Site-19. Due to the remote locations of some SCPF facilities, conventional military planners are to be told of a large-scale military drill. The regular army will serve as a means to distract SCPF forces while PENTAGRAM forces take Site-19.
Outline: The general assault on Site-19 will follow the start of military “drills” on other sites.
DECISION: REJECTED 3
.

Proposal 3

Author: Major R. Bahri & informant Executive 37Z
Summary: A coordinated strike against Site-19 with NSA 11, the Chaos Insurgency, and NSA 05, the Global Occult Coalition. PENTAGRAM will provide transport and arm the Insurgents. The informant has agreed to release information about completed and developing Foundation eigenweapons, which will be brought to the next GOC Physics Briefing. While the GOC engages SCPF, Insurgents will attempt to raid the site. PENTAGRAM will covertly enter and seize the assets.
Outline: A small, rapid, heavy force is to circumvent the few remaining SCPF forces not engaged with the Insurgents or the Coalition. This force is to quickly overwhelm any remaining defenses, seize the assets, and make a hasty retreat into the desert. They will make their way to Area-52 for the eventual safekeeping of the assets.
Additional Notes: It is of utmost importance that the GOC remain unaware of PENTAGRAM’s involvement on all fronts, including the arming of designated terrorists. It is also of high concern how our informant has access to high-level information without garnering suspicion. Caution should be exercised accordingly.
DECISION: ACCEPTED

EVENT NEG 55.88


Foreword: The following meeting was between NSA 1121, "Aetheric Engineers," a leading member of the GOC's engineering division. NSA 1121 are particularly close allies of NSA 2231, "Servants of the Silicon Nornir," a group that holds significant sway in the Council of 108. There is an expectation of close rapport between the two groups.

Parties Present:
Dr. Elizabeth Malcom — PENTAGRAM Representative
Divine-Tester Villa Berciu — Aetheric Engineers Representative

Purpose:
The event was informal, engineered to make the opposing party believe that Ms. Malcom was acting rogue and in her own interest.

Malcom: Good afternoon, Ms. Berciu.

Berciu: And bless you, Ms. Malcom. I haven't seen you in so long, my dear. How have you been?

Malcom: I'm doing better, Ma'am. Took your advice. Just concluded the divorce a week ago.

Berciu: Oh, how wonderful! You deserve a good man. Don't let anyone tell you any less, my dear.

Malcom: I promise, Ma'am. May we move on to business?

Berciu: Of course. I wasn't told much. Would you mind explaining what you require from us?

Suspected eigenweapon leaked by informant Executive 37Z

Malcom: We received some information from an informant last week. It appears to be blueprints for—if you'll forgive me—a weapon of some kind. From what our engineers have been able to ascertain, it is a kinetic weapon, but we aren't completely sure. We were hoping you could lend a hand.

Berciu: You know we don't do weapons, my dear. Personally, I frankly don't like the idea of weaponizing information. We know our motto.

Malcom: Of course, and I wouldn't have asked unless it was dire. If we can, let's keep this off the record. I am not allowed to disclose this, but the higher-ups believe it is to be used against our citizens. I… I just can't keep a lid on it anymore. I have nieces and nephews to consider. Please.

Berciu: I suppose a glance won't hurt. Let me have a look.

She stares at it for 4 minutes in silence. Her breathing grows more ragged, occasionally going still before resuming. At the 3-minute mark, her eyes widen.

Malcom: Ma'am?

Berciu does not look up.

Malcom: Ma'am? Ms. Berciu?!

Berciu shoots up.

Berciu: I'm sorry, my dear. Forgive me, but are you sure these are the plans you received? Please say it isn't so.

Malcom: It is. Is everything alright? Do you want water? Let me go call a nurse.

Berciu: No, I'm alright. Would you mind if I take a copy of sorts with me? I'd like to show some of my peers.

Malcom: Yes, of course. This one is a copy anyway. Keep it.

Berciu: May I ask why PENTAGRAM can't act on this?

Malcom: The machinery is in Foundation custody, and if we're right, they engineered it. No one will admit it, of course—no one wants to break any good ties we have left with them—but it's become an open secret.

Berciu: Do you have a location, sweetheart?

Malcom: You didn't hear this from me, but the facility known as Site-19. I have to go.
