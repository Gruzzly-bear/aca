SCP-9939 on Sněžka.

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9939 should be contained in a 30 m x 30 m x 15 m underground chamber located 10 meters underground, filled with rock and minerals. SCP-9939 must remain under constant surveillance to prevent escape. All entry points leading to SCP-9939 should always be guarded by two personnel.

When adding additional material to the containment unit every two weeks, SCP-9939 should be moved to a 7 m x 7 m x 7 m cube connected to its main chamber by one airlock. The process should be done in 15 minutes or less.

Test subjects should enter through 3 airlocks and 1 checkpoint to prevent contamination of the chamber. Testing on SCP-9939 should only occur during times when the entity is resting to prevent the endangerment of staff. Level 4 clearance required to access SCP-9939.

Following Incident-9939-4, D-Class must be disciplined and must take the Safety Test-9939 before candidates for testing are decided.

Description: SCP-9939 is a 5-meter-tall humanoid golem with iridescent eyes, made up of dirt, rock, and other minerals. Originally located at Site-228, it was moved to Site-19 because of the lack of space. SCP-9939 primarily consumes dirt, rock, and one live animal every two weeks.

SCP-9939 requires the iron in blood to keep itself alive. It is noted that SCP-9939 hunts by waiting and luring prey, a process that will result in SCP-9939 successfully consuming the blood of the prey in most cases. Additionally, SCP-9939 is capable of sensing heartbeats within 250 meters. SCP-9939 is also capable of reconstruction if destroyed.

It is also thought that, due to the amount of iron in SCP-9939, theoretically, it can release EMPs (electromagnetic pulses) and disrupt radio communications. The site's Electromagnetic Research Subdivision is currently testing solutions to this problem.

It is thought that SCP-9939 was created in the late 16th century by anomalous means. Sightings of SCP-9939 started in the late 1930s when a golem-like being was reported near the foothills of the Czech side of Mt. Sněžka. The sighting was brushed off by locals until the 1940s, when SCP-9939 moved to the attic of the Old-New Synagogue, and 2 weeks later, a Nazi officer mysteriously died in the attic. This is the first recorded hostile behavior of SCP-9939. No further sightings were documented or reported again for 52 years.

SCP-9939 was resighted in 1992 near the summit of Sněžka. This reached the attention of the Foundation, and Mobile Task Force Eta-77 ('Sphere Within Spheres") was deployed to recover the entity.

Upon arrival, SCP-9939 was observed hunting and eating a weasel. Although the majority of the carcass of the weasel was left behind, it was discovered in an analysis that 97% of the blood was missing, consumed by SCP-9939. Eta-77 quickly secured and successfully contained SCP-9939. The entity was kept at Site-228 for two months before being relocated to Site-19.

Rabbi █████ ██████ (later given amnestics) was questioned on the alleged "Jewish" golem, and the Foundation was allowed to contain SCP-9939 after it was concluded that SCP-9939 had come into contact with SCP-███ in 1930 due to records of ███████ behaviour, eliminating any association of the anomaly with Judaism. However, it is documented that SCP-9939 often communicates in Hebrew, and on occasion, in Latin.

Incident-9939-4

Throughout the course of SCP-9939's first 3 months in Site-19, the entity had breached containment three times. Although it chose to stay back when provided a live deer, dents were still visible on the hallways above and had to be repaired. During Test 9939-C7, SCP-9939 had become agitated when D-7345 had exhibited violent and destructive behavior towards the entity.

Update 3/4/2005: It has now been concluded that D-7345 had severe mental issues based on behavior with previous anomalies and SCP-9939.

View through the chest digital camera mounted on D-7345.

0:00 Dr. █████: Enter the chamber

(0:05) D-7345 enters the chamber and looks for SCP-9939.

(1:01) Dr. █████ SCP-9939 is to your left. Approach slowly.

(1:09) D-7345: 'Kay doc. Why so many plants?

(1:12) Dr. █████ I can't answer your questions

(1:19) D-7345: I know. Just messing. Same shit you tell me every time.

(1:25) D-7345 approaches SCP-9939.

(1:48) D-7345: Hey there, Big Chungus.

(1:55) Dr. █████ Stick to the questions on your hand. You don't want to go hungry again tonight.

(2:00) D-7345: (whispers) Shithead. (Normal) Fine.

(2:09) D-7345 taps SCP-9939. It slowly opens its eyes and turns to D-7345.

(2:20) SCP-9939: עוד אחד מהצ'קים שלהם T1

(2:45) D-7345: What the hell did you just say? Anyways… Uh… May fo ta mag iya?

(2:59) SCP-9939: █████ ████ אני בא מידי אדם. מבורך על ידי T2

(3:07) D-7345 picks up a rock in the containment chamber and throws it at SCP-9939.//

(3:09) D-7345: Jackass. I am supposed to get answers from you. Not cryptic messages.

D-7345 grabs a sharp stone and cracks part of SCP-9939

(3:12) Dr. █████ D-7345, stick to your task. You will have to be neutralized if directions aren't followed. Agitation of SCP-9939 cou-

(3:17) D-7345: Oh shut up!

(3:18) D-7345 proceeds to hit SCP-9939 again

(3:21) SCP-9939: זה מספיק! T3

(3:25) D-7345 climbs onto the cracked portion of SCP-9939 and keeps hitting it.

<End Log>

Due to D-7345's actions, SCP-9939 had responded violently and successfully emitted an EMP, disabling technology and electronics for all of Site-19.

View through cameras located in SCP-9939's containment chamber.

<Begin Log>

(3:29) SCP-9939: חרגת מהגבול, חייל מעבדה! T4

(3:34) SCP-9939 throws D-7345 onto the ground. The entity stands up, breaking the ceiling.

(3:34): D-7345: Fu-

(3:35) SCP-9939 crushes D-7345 and releases an electromagnetic pulse

<End Log>

View through Hallway 6L Camera 02

<Begin Log>

(5:23) Walls are visible crumbling as security guards run through the hallway. SCP-9939 crashes through a large doorway with hands covered with blood. Unintelligible yelling is heard as gunfire directed at SCP-9939 does no damage as it charges forward.

(5:58) Sgt. Denis Clain: Someone get the damn AT-4!

(6:48) SCP-9939: (Unintelligible)

(7:04) SCP-9939 is seen picking up Corp. Vance Flemming and throwing him at the security personnel, knocking them all down. The golem then rips the gate from the wall and drops it onto them. It then walks away slowly into Sector 2.

<End Log>

View through HelmCAM N7B

<Begin Log>

(24:09) MTF Nu-7 ("Hammer Down") enters through multiple points in the building. Their scans indicate that multiple anomalies are located in the East Wing.

(25: 17) Alpha: Echo and Delta, I need you two to get to the control room. See what we can prevent and restore.

(25:20) Echo and Delta are seen diverging from the group and entering a staircase to their right. The rest of the team continues panning the hallways as they hear the first crash. In an instant, rifles and lasers were pointed to the source of the crash. It was identified as SCP-9939.

(25:53) Bravo: AT-4!

The camera shakes as Bravo launches the AT-4. It hits the center of SCP-9939, demolishing it.

(26:00) Alpha Get them pieces and isolate them! We all saw the footage.

UPDATE 4/9/2020: Video log moments after 26:14 have been corrupted due to a second EMP from SCP-9939.

SCP-9939 was later temporarily recontained by having the pieces of the anomaly contained in different cubicles, which were filled with water and made of tempered glass. It was later returned to its original containment after four months of repair and renovation. However, the entity had later left a message carved into the cubicle that contained its head portion:

Cur rodentium laboratorii id fecit? Aegerne erat? Nunc vides quid accidit cum mecum male gesseris. Proximo tempore fortasse temptabo. T5

(Same text in Hebrew)

למה העכבר במעבדה עשה את זה? האם הוא היה חולה? עכשיו אתה רואה מה קורה כאשר אתה מתנהג רע כלפיי. בפעם הבאה, אולי אנסה. T6
