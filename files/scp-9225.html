Special Containment Procedures: Due to SCP-9225's antimemetic properties, few containment procedures are necessary. Noöspherics Division and security operatives under the influence of class-W mnestics are to patrol the area surrounding SCP-9225 in both seafaring and aerial vessels.

Any abnormal activity taking place within or surrounding SCP-9225 must be reported to lead researcher Wilmont as well as the Overseer Council.

Description: SCP-9225 is an island located in the Mediterranean sea, between Greece and Libya, measuring 80km in radius with an area of 20,000km2. The island is cloaked by an antimemetic effect, and as such, is entirely imperceptible and inaccessible to individuals not under the effects of mnestic drugs. The island itself has its topography partially warped in various locations, but most notably in the shoreline and its center. This can manifest via the bending of rocks to form sharp and uneven edges, as well as forcing the ground to bend at angles as steep as 90°. The environment, however, maintains the characteristics of a typical Mediterranean location, including non-anomalous biodiversity.

SCP-9225 is home to a variety of different anomalies, both living and inanimate. Close studies have also demonstrated that these anomalies are either unwilling, or unable to leave the boundraries of SCP-9225. Despite this, caution must be exercised for personnel safety. Study and cataloguing of all anomalies present within SCP-9225 is ongoing.

Spread across SCP-9225's terrain are multiple human settlements of varying size and composition, most of which are empty and intact. While the majority of settlements spread across the outer areas of the island are small towns or villages, in the island's center is a large city1, hereby designated SCP-9225-A, measuring roughly 5,000km2. Like the rest of the island's structures, the city is entirely undamaged. This is due to a secondary anomalous effect encompassing everything native to SCP-9225 which makes all objects indestructible, and in the case of living entities, infinitely reform once they've received enough damage. This effect has rendered research efforts highly arduous and extensive. No origin for this effect has been determined.

Based on early long distance imagery and analysis of SCP-9225-A and its surrounding settlements, it can be observed that the architecture used in their construction resembles a mix of various ancient civilizations. Mycenaean, Minoan, Mesopotamian, and Phoenician architecture have been found to be the most noticeable. Knowledge of how these different cultures could intertwine within SCP-9225 is unknown.

SCP-9225 was discovered on 2/18/2024 after Noöspherics Division operative Wendy Wright spotted the island while on vacation on a Mediterranean cruise line. When no other individuals could perceive the island, site command was notified and proper containment procedures were carried out.

Objective: Scout northern section of SCP-9225, report back with data.

[Begin Log, 2/20/2024]

14:27: Drone Alpha approaches the northern coastline of SCP-9225. Terrain is rough, with rows of abnormally large jagged rocks creating a makeshift barrier across the shore. The rocks making up the barrier extend 10 meters upwards and jut at various angles. To the right of the drone, a structure can be seen in the distance. The drone approaches.

14:35: The drone reaches a large port surrounded by residential and military buildings. The port resembles a large, conical cothon. Beside the exterior docks there are evenly placed wide ramps along the sides which extend under the water, with roads leading from the ramps into the city and other larger structures. Purpose unknown. Drone detects signs of large housing structures under the port. Movement under the water is detected, though visual contact is unfeasible.

15:01: Terrain resembles typical dry shrublands with hills bearing dense rock formations. Several non-anomalous mammals, avians, and reptilians spotted across the terrain. They do not act different from regular instances. Unidentified flying organisms spotted. They appear to be large insectoids, with four spider-like stone legs and two sets of chitinous wings. They can be seen drilling into the mountainous terrain and camouflaging into the stones with their exoskeletons of similar color, or swarming nearby organisms.

16:55: The drone passes through three small settlements, all bearing the same characteristics and architecture with small differences. All are intact and devoid of human habitation. Several unidentified organisms spotted during exploration, though they avoided contact with Drone Alpha.

18:48: Drone Alpha reaches a mountain of immense size when approaching the center of SCP-9225, with an estimated height of 3,500m above sea level. There are several openings carved into the base of the mountain as well as the upper regions. On approach, it can be seen that they are pathways leading into the mountain, with pillars and support extending inside. Drone progress is blocked by barred gates posted on each entrance.

22:39: Drone returns to base without issues.

[End Log]

Objective: Scout southern section of SCP-9225, report back with data.

[Begin Log, 2/20/2024]

14:21: Drone Beta arrives at the southern coast through another port similar to the one spotted by Drone Alpha. It can be surmised that there are several ports like these spread throughout the coast of SCP-9225. No unusual activity detected.

15:20: A particularly vegetated area leads into a biome composed entirely of stone. Despite this, there are multiple different forms of plant life made up of this stone material. This includes stone trees and a multitude of stone flowers and plants with different colored geodes making up their petals, leaves, and anthers.

16:11: The drone encounters a settlement, much larger than the ones found by Drone Alpha. This one looks to be a military fortress with a ziggurat-like structure serving as its center. In the area surrounding the fortress, as well as within, several animate skeletal entities can be seen roaming and patrolling the landscape. Entities resemble a variety of different extinct wildlife, some heavily altered, and others even wearing makeshift armor. The fortress shows signs of being made to accommodate these anomalies and other militaristic equipment. Several roads extend from the fortress.

17:49: The large mountain spotted by Drone Alpha can be seen. From Drone Beta's perspective, a large, complex city is built in the area beneath the mountain, as well as the higher areas. Parts of SCP-9225-A are built into the mountain, with the metropolis surrounded by it on all sides save for the south. The city is multi-layered, with some of the higher areas' structures blending into the mountain. Ziggurats and large halls are spread across the entire expanse, separated by dozens of residential and mercantile buildings, topped by a large palace at the highest point of the city.

21:53: The drone returns to base without complications.

[End Log]

Objective: Scout eastern section of SCP-9225, report back with data.

[Begin Log, 2/20/2024]

14:36: Drone Charlie arrives on the eastern coast, beside another port town. The drone spots signs of multiple structures of varying sizes under the water and connected to the island. In conjunction, the underwater structures resemble a settlement. Several humanoid shapes can be spotted moving rapidly between the structures.

15:00: Several small storm circles move across the landscape. In between each strike of lightning, flocks of unknown avian organisms fly in unison in the storms' centers.

16:12: Several villages identified, no further anomalies detected.

17:34: A large temple lies surrounded by dozens of domestic buildings. On the outskirts there are several pens and fields of vegetation. Some are filled with non-anomalous animals, while others lie empty.

17:55: The drone reaches the center of SCP-9225. Passage into SCP-9225-A without going through the gates on the mountain or through the southern sector is ill-advised.

22:06: Drone Charlie returns to base without further incident.

[End Log]

Objective: Scout western section of SCP-9225, report back with data.

[Begin Log, 2/20/2024]

14:45: Drone Delta approaches the western coast, which is heavily forested for several kilometers. The forest is obscured, with the drone's headlight being unable to penetrate the darkness beyond two meters. Movement detected amidst the dense vegetation. Drone instructed to fly over the area.

17:13: Drone Delta spots another large settlement a few kilometers from the forest covering much of the west side of SCP-9225. There are three pyramid structures, albeit smaller than those found by Alpha and Beta. Outside the town there is a concentration of buildings near a cave leading underneath a hillside. There are several roads spreading from the settlement further into SCP-9225.

18:06: Transmission is lost after a projectile from an unidentified source strikes down Drone Delta.

[End Log]

It's an entire ecosystem of anomalies intermixed with the remnants of a civilization that, for all we know, seemed quite dexterous in the field of anomalous phenomena, more so than its contemporaries. Expedition Log

[Begin Log]

Feed opens upon a small Foundation vessel. The weather is cloudy, with waves splashing the deck of the ship. The shore of SCP-9225 can be seen rapidly approaching. The Lima Team members make final preparations.

Lima-1: Everybody's feed up and running?

All team members nod in unison. A large wave crashes into the ship, drenching several of the agents.

Lima-4: Are you fu-

Lima-5: Come on…

Lima-7: Shit, that's cold!

Command: Prepare for deployment. Arrival on SCP-9225 in T-minus thirty seconds.

The agents move hurriedly and position themselves near the front of the vessel. Each has a backpack full of supplies to set up a temporary base of operations.

Command: We'll have our ships close by for when you send the signal. Once you've ensured full control over B1, we'll send in the rest of the personnel.

Lima-1: Understood command, ready for deployment.

The ship enters the port and approaches the right docks. When it closes the distance, the agents jump onto one of the ramps. Once all members are off, the boat leaves to reconvene with the larger ships. The agents look around.

Lima-3: <whistles> Damn…

Lima-8: Impressive right?

Lima-3:Yeah. I've seen my fair share of anomalous ruins, but this is something else.

Lima-7: I'm just glad it ain't Sarkic. Trust me, if the walls don't eat you, you'll be smelling like a fresh corpse for weeks.

// Lima team ascends the ramp and walks along the docks. On their right are several buildings which appear to be made of limestone. On their left, several ships are tied to masts along the canal.//

Lima-1: Remember to look out for traps. Never know with these things.

Lima-2: I'm more worried about the other anomalies. You see the shit in those recordings?

Lima-5: That's why they sent us here. Coast seemed safest.

Lima-2: Still, like cap said, never know with these things.

Lima team continues to walk along the harbor, eventually reaching a large building where the canal ends and several ships are stored.

Lima-1: Command, we've reached the end of the docks. No sign of opposition so far.

Command: Copy, continue the mission. Attempt to reach the main gubernatorial building.

The squad continues past the cothon and into the city. Despite the wide streets, there are structures everywhere, all unaltered. Spread across the streets are shops, carts, chariots, and different types of pottery. The team keeps their guns drawn. One hour later, they reach a large, palace-like structure. It is rectangular, with pillars lining the inside. Intricate carvings of seafaring vessels, soldiers, and underwater humanoids with weapons are spread throughout the stone surfaces and ceramic pots decorate the hall. They walk inside the main room. There are several passages that lead further into the building.

Lima-1: Alright, Three, Four, and Six, you start setting up in here. Five and Eight, check the perimeter. Two and Seven, we clear out the rest of the palace.

The agents nod and disperse to fulfill their respective missions. Lima-1,-2, and -7 move to the back of the room where a small, plain throne is located. Above the throne there is a large mural, it depicts a regal figure, dressed in armor with a bronze tinge and wielding an ornate longsword of the same color in the right hand, and a white scepter with an eye in the left. Lima-1 signals for the team to split. Lima-2 and -7 go down the right hall, while he goes down the left.

Command: Lima Five, Lima Eight, report.

Lima-5: Everything's alright on my end. No signs of threats so far.

Lima-8: Same here, but I did come across a large garden near the back. All the plants seem to be fine, no overgrowth or anything.

Command: We'll notify the researchers about the observation. Continue your duties.

As Lima-1 walks down the corridor, a sudden noise echoes through the palace. He whirls around to look behind him, but there is nothing there.

Lima-1: Everyone, report. What was that?

Lima-6: Well, Lima-4 accidentally knocked over one of the vases while setting up the light attachments… and it doesn't have any damage.

Lima-4: Not only that… but the reason I tripped, is because the walls won't budge. None of the nails I try to hammer in go through.

Lima-3: Hold on, let me try something.

A gunshot explodes from the main room, followed by silence.

Lima-3: Well, something's definitely off. The shot did nothing to the vase, still as new as how we found it.

Lima-1: Okay, put the vase back in its place and set up what you can, however you can. We'll leave the rest for the coats to figure out.

Lima-1 continues walking down the dark corridor, coming across several doors. He opens each one, revealing either ornate bedrooms, dining halls, or servant's quarters. On the final door, Lima-1 opens to reveal an armory. Inside the armory are several typical weapons of the era, but on the far wall, hung to the wall, lies a gold lyre decorated with bright topaz gems.

Lima-1: Command, have eyes on a possible anomalous weapon.

Lima-2: We um.. also found the library. Or at least we think it is.

Lima-4: We've set up on our end, or at least what we can. B1 camp is set and ready!

Command: Understood. Lima-1, do we have an all clear to make landfall?

Lima-1 walks back to the main palace room. The rest of the agents, bar Lima-2 and -7, are present. Lima-8 walks up to Lima-1.

Lima-8: Outside's all clear captain. No hostiles in sight.

Lima-1: Alright then. Command, make ready for landfall. You've got the all clear.

[End Log]

Expedition Log

Preface: Due to the continued presence of multiple prehistoric entities surrounding L.O.I.B2, a division of MTF Phi-2 (Clever Girls) was provided with class-W mnestics and tasked to, alongside Team Lima, contain or disperse the anomalous entities and secure access to L.O.I.B2. Date: 3/01/2024

Expedition Team: Agent Rodriguez(L2), Agent Asent(L3), Agent Ortiz(L4), Agent Rivera(L5), Agent Farraday(L6), Agent Gomez(L7), Agent Towers(L8), Agent Holland(P2), Agent Gines(P3), Agent Jewels(P4), Agent Zultan(P5), Agent Pimentel(P6)

The sun is in the middle of the sky, with clouds of dust obscuring 30% visibility as the teams pass through the rocky bushlands. Lima-1 drives an armored Jeep, with Lima-2 on the passenger seat and Lima-3 and -4 readying equipment in the back. Lima-1 looks to his right. There are two armored trucks with large containment cages on their beds, varying in sizes of 3 to 5 meters. They are being driven by the members of MTF Phi-2. On the left is the rest of Lima squad in their own armored Jeep.

Phi-2-1: Coming up on Beta two, everyone keep a lookout.

Through the dust, the top of a large stone ziggurat becomes visible from behind fortified stone walls. A giant stone archway gives passage into the fortress.

Phi-2-5: I see 'em! East!

From behind the walls, several skeletal entities emerge charging towards the agents. There are 4 entities in total. The two largest entities resemble mastodon skeletons, albeit with several parts of their body protected by metal armor, and the two others appear to be a tethyshadros and a type of saber-toothed feline.

The Phi-2 squadron separates from team Lima, staying back to unload the cages. Lima team continues to engage with the entities.

Lima-1: Alright everyone, you know the drill. Immobilize, then contain. Four, Seven, you're up!

Lima-4 opens the jeep's sunroof, carrying a portable missile launcher. On the other jeep, Lima-7 does the same. A few moments later, two rockets fly across the landscape, landing close to the entities. The blast breaks apart one of the smaller entities, while knocking down another, but the mastodons keep charging forward. The rest of the agents open fire on the anomalies.

The two vehicles are able to dodge the oncoming collisions and continue suppressive fire on the entities. While the armor receives no damage, the areas of exposed bone slowly begin to break apart. The entities attempt to knock away the vehicles, but the agents are able to maneuver out of the attacks.

After 2 minutes of continuous sustained firing, each of the mastodons loses one leg, causing them to collapse.

Lima-1: Phi-1, three targets immobilized, one terminated. You are clear for containment.

Phi-2-1: Copy that Lima One, we're-

One of the downed skeletal instances lets out a loud roar, followed by a distant rumbling. From the fortress' gates, a large skeletal specimen resembling a mansourasaurus slowly lumbers towards the team. Close behind, a wave of several dozen raptor-like entities2 rapidly approach team Lima.

Lima-5: Holy shit!

Lima-8: Captain, what's the plan?

Lima-1: Phi One, how long before you can get containment ready?

Phi-2-1: The small one's too far, but if you can buy us a few minutes, we can get at least one of the elephants ready to go.

Lima-1: Copy. Lima Five, follow me.

The two vehicles make a hard left, heading towards a hillside in the distance. Lima agents fire their weapons towards the crowd of entities, prompting them to follow away from Phi-2.

Lima-1: Delta-45, once we come back around, we're going to need you to create as much distance between us and the entities as possible.

Delta-45-1: Understood Lima One, making our way to you.

The two vehicles reach the hill and begin rapidly ascending.

Lima-1: Ready up the next round of missiles! We're blasting away as many of these fuckers as we can.

As they're about to crest the hill, the jeeps make a hard left, executing a U turn back towards the fortress. During the turn, Lima-4 and Lima-7 fire two more missiles into the crowd, shattering several of the skeletal entities and causing the rest to momentarily hesitate.

In that moment of hesitation, Delta-45 arrives on the scene, firing a barrage of gunfire and explosives which either destroys or disperses the rest of the entities. The Mansourasaurus attempts to reach Delta-45, but all the agents maneuver around it, eventually taking it down, but not destroying it.

Lima-3: Woo! Like fish in a barrel!

Lima-1: Thanks for the assist Delta. Standby for further action.

Delta-45-1: Not a problem Lima. Ya'll just get your sorry asses back to Phi.

Lima-1: Phi One, what's your status?

Phi-2-1: Well, we managed to corral these sons-a-guns into the cages, and even managed to snag ourselves a little extra. Those two that you blew up a bit ago? They reformed. Nothing we couldn't handle though.

Delta-45-1: Yeap, same thing happening over here.

Lima-1 looks back, all of the skeletons and their shattered pieces can be seen slowly reforming back together into complete instances. Before they can do much though, they are driven away or injured by Delta-45.

Delta-45-1: Don't worry about over here, we got these handled. Just get to the Fortress and let command know to bring more units for the takeover. And a bigger cage for long neck over 'ere.

Team Lima reaches Phi-2, which have now captured the first four entities that attacked the agents. The entities attempt to bite the agents and crash against the bars but are unable to break out of containment. Lima-3 looks out of the window as Phi-2-1 approaches.

Lima-3: Just another day?

Phi-2-1: <sighs> Just another day.

Lima-1: Command, we've secured the perimeter of Beta Two and dispersed all known hostiles. Requesting additional units for infiltration of the structure itself.

Command: Copy that Lima One, remain on standby for further action.

[End Log]

Documents suggest that I̅X̅-1 instances were created through a thaumaturgic ritual involving the blood of several key figures in SCP-9225 culture, I̅X̅-2, and other currently unknown substances. Tests have shown that instances share a hivemind-like consciousness which likely contributes to their ability to communicate and work in tandem with one another, likely a result of I̅X̅-2 used during their creation.

I̅X̅-5 are anomalous humanoid constructs composed primarily out of wood, tree bark, and other tree components. Their sizes vary, but are often between the 1.5m-2m range. The bottom half of their body is made up of various roots that can be used as independent limbs for either movement or management of items. The upper half of their body is humanoid, with a canopy of branches and leaves growing out of their head, and a featureless face, with the exception of two glowing green eyes.

Based on observations, tests, and historical documentations, I̅X̅-5 instances were built primarily for menial labor such as farm work or house duties. While the method of their creation, as well as how they were instructed, is unknown, various discovered texts imply a strong connection to I̅X̅-6 entities.

I̅X̅-6 entities are anomalous constructs built out of various types of stone. They measure between 7-8 meters in height, with large, thick, and durable arms and legs. I̅X̅-6 instances lack a head and instead possess a large yellow runic eye carved into the center of their main body mass, which is roughly spherical.

These entities have incredible strength and defense, able to withstand most forms of firearms, and are primarily used as guardians/soldiers of SCP-9225-A. The method of their creation and methods of control is unknown, but are believed to be tied to I̅X̅-55.

I̅X̅-7 are an anomalous species of avian organisms which can be found across various environments within SCP-9225. These avian creatures measure 30cm long, with three sets of wings which each measure 45cm in wingspan. I̅X̅-7 instances are usually white and grey in coloration, with red and orange eyes, and a sharp yellow beak.

When threatened, I̅X̅-7 instances will rapidly flap their wings in order to create small gusts of wind capable of reaching 45kmh. Once the victim is disoriented, they will either flee, or attempt to overwhelm them if there are other instances nearby. They will use this same ability when hunting. Instances are omnivorous, but primarily feed on fruits, rodents, or other small organisms.

I̅X̅-7 instances are often found in flocks, and are rarely ever seen alone unless they are hunting, or searching for a mate, at which point they will leave the flock and try to mate with an instance from another flock.

I̅X̅-8 are agressive humanoid entities whose body are composed entirely of animate pieces of armor. Measuring between 1.5m-2m in height, these entities' bodies, or the armor they possess, are composed of a beryllium bronze alloy capable of absorbing thaumaturgical energy used to try and attack it. Their bodies are resistant to damage, but with enough sustained fire, can be temporarily terminated. The armor of I̅X̅-8 instances is of a bronze coloration, with several repeating designs across the body and a carved human face in their helmet. Expressions can vary between instances.

I̅X̅-8 instances serve as the primary defense force of SCP-9225-A, and will attack any individual on sight if it perceives them as a threat to SCP-9225-A. They often wield a variety of melee weapons also composed of beryllium bronze. Even if the weapon is taken away, instances are capable of taking and transmuting nearby materials into a new weapon.

The origin of I̅X̅-8 instances is unknown, as records indicate that, when the Seaenid empire still existed, their forces were composed of entirely of living individuals.

I̅X̅-9 is a spear composed primarily of a gold and copper alloy. This spear has been altered to harness an anomalous substance injected directly into a system of carved lines along its shaft and blade, which renders it capable of producing electrical energy. This energy can be stored or discharged at will and its intensity depends on the amount of energy stored prior to a discharge.

Based on textual evidence, the substance within I̅X̅-9 seems to originate from an anomalous species of marine fauna native to SCP-9225. Due to its apparent rarity, however, only a limited amount of I̅X̅-9 instances were produced, several of which have been found by Foundation personnel.

I̅X̅-10 are an anomalous species of insectoid creatures commonly found in the more rock-filled areas of SCP-9225. Instances vary in coloration, but are typically grey, brown, black or variations of these three. Specimens are primarily carnivorous, and will hunt in pacts for their prey, at which point they will attempt to drill through their targets with their legs and feed on their innards through the use of their tongue.

I̅X̅-11 is a thaumaturgic ritual capable of summoning one or more sandstorms of varying levels of intensity. When summoned, these sandstorms are under the control of the user and can be manipulated according to their will, although it takes focus to maintain them for long periods of time.

So it is I stand against my enemy one last time. The bones of the Unseen lie before me, wretched things. I never thought I'd see them again. Yet, despite my disdain, it is necessary. PROJECT PERPETUA PROPOSAL

Purpose: Discover a method of immortalizing the Foundation and its essential personnel so that the mission of Secure, Contain, and Protect, can be efficiently carried out for the foreseeable future.

Methodology: Phase 1 of Project Perpetua will involve the analysis of SCP-9225's "immortality" effect through the use of Foundation Kant counters, Conceptual Energy Analyzers, and Antimemetic Field Disruptors. The same shall be done for all SCP-9225-Ω instances in order to understand the impacts of SCP-9225's effect on living entities. Countermeasures against the erasure of sentience and sapience, as well as the imposition of anomalous qualities and changes based on collective beliefs will also be developed.

Once research is complete, Phase 2 will consist of attempts at replicating the same effect with Foundation resources. Tests will consist of first applying SCP-9225's effects on objects and organisms not present within SCP-9225. Once the indestructibility and regenerative properties of these tests are verified, the following test will verify the consequences on D-class personnel. Once these tests are also confirmed successful, the last stage of Phase 2 will commence, which consists of attempting to apply SCP-9225's qualities on concepts and the resulting reactions of all elements falling under the category of said concept. All data will be recorded and stored in backup antimemetic resistant deepwells in case of residual antimemetic effects contaminating the tests.

Phase 3 will begin once all testing is found to be successful. At this point, a refined procedure for the implementation of SCP-9225's effects on the Foundation will be developed. Following approval, a list of personnel deemed essential for the continuation of the Foundation's mission shall be discussed, subsequently culminating in the full, irreversible execution of the procedure.
