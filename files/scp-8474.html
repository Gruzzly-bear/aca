SCP-8474

Washington Rehabilitation Center circa 2028

Item #: SCP-8474

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: The maternal tradition is to be encouraged by the Department of Continuity, and a webcrawler has been set to monitor the activity of hospitals and therapeutic centers to detect possible victims of SCP-8474.

All victims are to be relocated to a rehabilitation center and given appropriate disciplinary action. Persistent inadequate behavior is to be followed by indefinite sedation, and health check-ups are to be conducted daily and restricted to one man.

Mnestics are to be dosed following reeducation.

A subject pregnant in both her mind and her womb in a recent study. Parturition of the former was successful.

Description: SCP-8474 is a mind-affecting phenomenon known by its unprecedented impact on the world's birth rate in the past few centuries. It is mainly characterized by a biological female's unwillingness to accept a gestation.

In order to accelerate the recovery from all the damage caused by SCP-8474, the Department of Biology and Medical Science is now working on the development of new forms of childbirth.
