SCP-5558

Item #: ğ’«

Object Class: Keter

Disambiguation: ğ’« may refer to either part of the interconnected anomaly described below.

  • ğ’« when described in physical situations or as performing actions, refers to the individual.
  • ğ’« when described with regards to memetic effects or written/spoken transmission, refers to the word.

Special Containment Procedures: Due in part to ğ’«'s docility and compliance with the Foundation, ğ’«'s containment has been adapted to allow for as much autonomy as possible, including janitorial duties and scheduling interviews/therapy.

Though information about the word ğ’« is largely self-normalizing, it is to be removed from public access when possible, and should be limited in Foundation documents to those pre-approved by the Department of Miscommunications.

ğ’«'s mother, Penelope Yore, is on close watch, and is allowed weekly calls with ğ’«, though precautions such as a delayed line must be taken to ensure that no information about the Foundation is able to transfer. ğ’« is aware that Ms. Yore's knowledge of the situation is incomplete, and has thus far been able to adapt to Foundation efforts consistently.

Description: ğ’« (formerly Nudah Borvitch) is an 18-year-old human with a drastically disparate cranial structure when compared with the average individual. ğ’«'s otherwise standard human body leads into a fleshy, conical neck supporting a gargantuan head with 50-100 small orifices composed of various facial tissues including skin, cartilage, adipose, and bone. These orifices are largely symmetrical, with minor discrepancies across the midline of the face, and are in a constant state of flux, having new ones generated while the cranial flesh simultaneously absorbs existing ones.

ğ’« also refers to the word "ğ’«", which displays a number of memetic qualities with regards to its transmission, both auditory and visual. ğ’«, the individual, cannot be referred to by any name or direct identifier other than "ğ’«". When written or otherwise transferred visually, all direct reference to ğ’« is limited to the word "ğ’«". However, when spoken, the word is pronounced identically to a separate word from the English language. A spoken rendition of the word "ğ’«" may be heard below.

Update (07-18-19): While conscious, ğ’« is able to manifest a variety of objects, though this appears to be entirely unintentional on ğ’«'s part, both in the frequency and content of the manifestations.
