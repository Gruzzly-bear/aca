Item #: SCP-9753

White Ceramic Mug

Object Class: Standard

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9753 is to be stored in a 5x5x5 meter3 containment chamber within Site-357. SCP-9753 is not to be subject to external influences.

The SCP-9753 chamber is to contain a 5 x 5 array of industry-standard white ceramic mugs, oriented identically upon distinct 1 meter tall white pillars. The mugs are described as follows:

Daily testing is necessary for containment.1

A teapot is to be held within the containment chamber.

Description: SCP-9753 is an industry-standard white ceramic mug. SCP-9753 remains within 1% deviation of a sample of 24 similar mugs.
