Special Containment Procedures: A piece of A4 paper with SCP-9207 printed on it is to be locked under a 5-step verification locker in Room-309, Hallway-85 in Site-19. All personnel must input Level 6 credentials to access SCP-9207. Any personnel under Level 6 who have gossiped, heard, or attempting to enter SCP-9207's chamber or locker are to be terminated by θ-61 ("Carbonation-Based Lifeforms"). Any information about SCP-9207 outside of Foundation must be neutralized and subjects succumbing to SCP-9207's infohazardous effects must be terminated before the 48 hour mark. SCP-9207 has already wiped out 99% 100% of the human population after a IHK-class "Infohazard Apocalypse" scenario. Special Containment Procedures at this time are irrelevant

Description: SCP-9207 is the claimed "recipe" of the world-famous Coca-Cola brand carbonated beverage. SCP-9207 is highly infohazardous, and within 48 hours of gaining knowledge of SCP-9207, subjects will die from increased heartrate, ruptured arteries or subject's blood being replaced with an unknown chemical1. The following is an exact 1:1 recreation of SCP-9207 with infohazards redacted:

Addendum-9207-01: SCP-9207 was created by using an AI to order ingredients under the disguise of a restaurant in █████████, Missouri. Each section of SCP-9207 was read aloud by a text-to-speech computer to 5 D-class personnel. When finished, the only D-class not been affected by SCP-9207's infohazardous effects was asked with pouring the mixture2 into a glass Coca-Cola brand carbonated beverage bottle3. When consumed, subject shown improvements in fine and gross motor skills, lack of rest and an increased heartrate of 170 bpm. Subject was found dead approximately 48 hours after consumption of SCP-207-1-Y. with autopsy proving subject had died from heart failure. A second test has been approved by O5 Command.

Addendum-9207-02: SCP-9207 was recreated same as the previous test. Subject showed similar signs as to Test-9207-01, and died 48 hours later from a ruptured artery near subject's coccyx. SCP-9207 has been believed to be the recipe of SCP-207, and further investigation has been done to prove this theory
