SCP-8552

Item #: SCP-8552

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: Logistical Site-180 has been placed under indefinite quarantine, pending removal by the active Site Director. To limit the spread, all 123 site personnel must undergo weekly medical examination. A section of Site-180’s medical facilities has been sectioned off to this end. All SCP-8552 sightings must be immediately reported to Site Task Force Beta-2 (“Pest Control”) for extermination. Updates on the epidemic and general status of Site-180 are attached in Addendum 8552.1.

Several SCP-8552 instances pictured in Researcher Williams's bed.

Description: SCP-8552 refers to a colony of Cimex lectularius1 currently infesting Site-180's residential sector. After being bitten by an SCP-8552 instance individuals develop atypical symptoms, including:

  • Dermatillomania
  • Aversion to bright lights
  • Spontaneous mutism
  • Lack of appetite and thirst
  • Insomnia
  • Tinnitus2
  • Loss of fine motor control
  • Excessive weight loss (~10% of body mass)

Despite the severity of these symptoms, infected personel report minimal pain and no fatalities have occurred.

Addendum 8552.1: Site-180 Updates

Update 19/04/25: 26 staff members confirmed infected. Bedding is collected sitewide and transferred to Acroamatic Abatement for physical and metaphysical obliteration. To combat productivity loss as a result of sleep deprivation, sedatives and Foundation-use amphetamines are distributed amongst the infected.

Update 02/05/25: 59 staff members confirmed infected. Due to growing concern over space, healthy non-essential personnel are innoculated using SCP-8552. Quarantine zone expanded to include employee workstations. Alongside routine replacements of employee bedding, Beta-2 patrol the quarantine area and destroy any organic matter lost by the infected.

Update 09/05/25: 77 staff members confirmed infected. Researcher Williams’s corpse is discovered in their room, having undergone extensive decomposition. This marks the first known fatality from SCP-8552. Beta-2 dispatched for on-site cremation and destruction of all SCP-8552 instances within the remains.

Update 10/05/25: 78 staff members confirmed infected. Following Researcher Williams's actions, all patrols of the quarantine zone are cancelled. Beta-2 reassigned to maintain isolation; use of lethal force authorized by Site Director Akran. Objects within the noospheric definition of “bed” are annihilated.

Update 21/05/25: 121 staff members confirmed infected. Quarantine zone is expanded sitewide; excluding Site Director’s office. Neighbouring Site-██ receives requisition order for a two-seat extraction vehicle.

Update 22/05/25: 122 staff members confirmed infected. Site-██ requisition order cancelled. On-site nuclear warhead is primed for detonation.

Update 23/05/25: 123 staff members confirmed infected. On-site nuclear warhead deactivated. Quarantine is officially lifted.

Update 03/06/25: 134 Foundation employees confirmed infected. Following Director Akran's petition, Overseer Council votes on the reclassification of SCP-8552.
