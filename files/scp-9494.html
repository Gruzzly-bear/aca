SCP-9494

Who knew playing Jenga could save a worldwide disaster from an ancient tower.

Fermentating

How long has it been…? Have my emperors at last arrived, or do these walls still keep me waiting in vain?

Have they at last ascended into the heavens? Do they still falter upon the earth?

Long have I laid beneath these sands… I have been forgotten, I am replied with silence. Now I rise, story by story, to meet the sky once promised. Let the sands fall from my walls, for I was not built to slumber, but to stand, and to reach where Heaven once beckoned!

RISE!
